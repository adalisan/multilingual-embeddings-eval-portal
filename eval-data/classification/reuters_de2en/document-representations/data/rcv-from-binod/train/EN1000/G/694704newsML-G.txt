England selectors on Sunday called up seamers Dean Headley and Mike Smith in a squad of 14 for the third test against Australia starting at Old Trafford on Thursday . 
Left-arm spinner Phil Tufnell is also included along with the 11 who played in the first two tests . 
England lead the six-match series 1-0 . 
fast Bowler Devon Malcolm is nursing a bruised foot but is expected to be fit by Thursday . 
squad : Mike Atherton ( captain ) , Mark Butcher , Alec Stewart , Nasser Hussain , Graham Thorpe , John Crawley , Mark Ealham , Robert Croft , Darren Gough , Andrew Caddick , Devon Malcolm , Phil Tufnell , Dean Headley , Mike Smith .
