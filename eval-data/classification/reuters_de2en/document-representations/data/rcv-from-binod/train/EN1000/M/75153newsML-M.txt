the Central Reserve Bank of Peru said on Wednesday it accepted bids for 42-day certificates of Deposit ( CDs ) worth 56.7 million soles at an effective average annual rate of 14.46 percent during a bank auction BCQZ . 
the CDs have a September 26 starting date . 
the accepted bids had a minimum rate of 14.00 percent and a maximum rate 14.53 percent , the bank said . 
at the last auction on Tuesday , 42-day CDs drew an average yield of 14.57 percent . 
at Wednesday &apos;s auction , the bank said it received bids on the CDs totalling 134.2 million soles . 
the bank had offered 50.0 million soles worth of CDs . 
the average effective annual interest rate for the certificates is used as an informal indicator for interest rates in the Peruvian financial system .
