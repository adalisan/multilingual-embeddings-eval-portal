Argentine cash maize closed steady to weaker in slow trade at the Buenos Aires Cereals Exchange Thursday , with export demand absent and leaving the market to local consumption , traders said . 
FAS elevator maize lost $ 1 for delivery to the port of Rosario to $ 99 , but was unchanged elsewhere . 
cash soybeans slipped $ 5 for Rosario on weak crusher demand . 
Rosario sunflower seed was unchanged at $ 218 , but demand was mainly limited to a few smaller crushers .
