Sweden &apos;s central bank , the Riksbank , set a one-week repurchase agreement operation at a steady , fixed 4.10 percent on Tuesday . 
the operation will run from August 13 to August 20 with bids due by 0800 GMT on August 12 . 
the repo rate is the bank &apos;s key tool for controlling market money liquidity . 
it has been unchanged since December 17 when it was lowered 25 basis points . 
the central bank &apos;s other key interest rates , the deposit and lending rates , stand at 3.75 percent and 5.25 percent , respectively .
