Swedish opposition to Economic and Monetary Union ( EMU ) is growing with more than four out of ten Swedes against the idea , a survey by pollster Temo showed on Saturday . 
less than a third of Swedes are positive about the possibility of joining a single European currency and the rest are undecided . 
the survey was conducted during November and December and showed opposition towards EMU is increasing . 
in October 38 percent of the 1,000 people questioned for a similar survey were opposed to EMU but this increased to 44 percent by the end of the year . 
the latest poll also showed that men are more positive towards EMU than women and support for EMU is stronger in bigger towns and cities than in the country . 
the Temo survey said its results also showed the people with a higher eduction were more in favour of EMU .
