Reuters said its 10-year tax-exempt general obligation bond index rose 12 basis points to 5.22 percent Thursday from 5.10 percent last week . 
the figure is the highest to date for the six-week-old index , which hit its low point on Aug 8 at 4.93 percent . 
calculated each Thursday , the index represents the non-weighted average yield on 15 state and local municipal bonds of 10-year maturity and ratings ranging from A-minus to AAA . 
the issues are from Maryland ; North Carolina ; Texas Permanent School Fund ; Columbus , Ohio ; Fairfax County , va . ; Connecticut ; Florida Board of Education ; Wisconsin ; Atlanta , Georgia ; Shelby County , Tenn . ; California ; New York State ; Pennsylvania ; Dade County , Fla. and Tucson , Ariz .
