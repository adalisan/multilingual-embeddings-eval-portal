Budapest , Nov 7 , Reuter - Hungary &apos;s producer price index rose 1.4 percent month on month in September and 20.4 percent year on year , the Central Statistical Office said on Thursday . 
in August the monthly rise was 1.7 percent and year on year was 20.9 percent . 
domestic sales prices month on month were up 1.2 percent in September and 21.6 percent year on year . 
export prices rose 1.9 percent month on month and 17.2 percent year on year .
