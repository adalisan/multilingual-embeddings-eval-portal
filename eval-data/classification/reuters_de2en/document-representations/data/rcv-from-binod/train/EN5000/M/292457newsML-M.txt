the Tokyo commodity Exchange ( TOCOM ) said on Tuesday it would raise the margin requirement on its January rubber futures contract by 50,000 yen per trading lot , effective from Wednesday . 
the rise will bring the total January requirement to 127,750 yen , TOCOM said in a statement . 
the increase is intended to cool overheated trade . 
the requirement on other rubber contracts will be unchanged at 52,500 yen , TOCOM said .
