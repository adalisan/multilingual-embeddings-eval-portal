the Slovak Finance Ministry will auction two unspecified tranches of treasury bills on Wednesday , the National Bank of Slovakia ( NBS ) said on Monday . 
the NBS said one tranche of four-week T-bills and one with a maturity of 40 weeks would be on sale . 
in the last successful auction of four-week T-bills on October 23 , the average yield hit 9.206 percent . 
the ministry has not held a 40-week T-bill auction yet this year . 
but in the last successful auction of T-bills with a similar maturity were 39-week T-bills issued on November 14 , whose average yield hit 9.986 percent .
