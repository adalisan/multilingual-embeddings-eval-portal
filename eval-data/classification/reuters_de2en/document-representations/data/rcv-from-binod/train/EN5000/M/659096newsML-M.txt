the Czech National Bank ( CNB ) on Friday set a one-week reverse repo tender to drain funds from the market at a maximum rate of 29 percent , down from 31 percent in a similar operation on Wednesday . 
the central bank has been holding several tenders on almost a daily basis over the past few weeks in order to drain liquidity from the market and help support the crown . 
the CNB has restricted banks from access to Lombard credit , after raising it to 50 percent from 14 percent . 
it has also informally limited foreign banks &apos; access to the money market and raised its discount rate to 13 percent from 10.5 percent .
