Nebraska feedlot cattle markets near midday Tuesday were not trading , with bids at $ 103.00 per cwt on a dressed basis being passed by owners pricing cattle at $ 105.00 to $ 106.00 , market sources said . 
there also were no trades reported on a live basis , with bids at $ 63.00 passed by owners asking $ 65.00 , they said . 
a storm overnight Monday left seven to 10 inches of wet snow from central to northeastern Nebraska , the sources said . 
the snow could slow performance at feedlots , but cattle were not expected to be overly stressed because temperatures were not seen dropping too low , they said .
