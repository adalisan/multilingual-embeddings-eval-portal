Vietnam will offer 70 billion Dong ( $ 6 million ) worth of one-year Treasury notes at an auction on Wednesday , an official at the State Bank of Vietnam said on Tuesday . 
at the last sale of government paper , on March 19 , the central bank sold 50 billion Dong worth of one-year Treasury notes to domestic institutions at an annual interest rate of 12 percent . 
the government held five Treasury paper auctions so far this year , through which a total of 178.4 billion Dong worth of Treasury stock has been issued .
