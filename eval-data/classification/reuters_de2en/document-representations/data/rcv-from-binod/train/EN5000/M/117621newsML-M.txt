Bangladesh tea prices fell at the weekly auction on Tuesday , brokers said . 
buyers for Poland , Pakistan , Russia and Afghanistan favoured mainly bold and small brokens and fannings . 
local blenders were also active but operated at a lower level . 
more than 855,270 kg of teas were sold at the auction at an average price of 43.91 taka , down 0.44 from the previous sale . 
the next auction will be held on October 22 with an offer of 1.24 million kg .
