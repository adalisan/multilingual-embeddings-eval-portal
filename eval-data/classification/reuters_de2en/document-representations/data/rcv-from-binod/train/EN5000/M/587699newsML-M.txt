the European Union on Thursday awarded a tariff cut of 39.25 Ecus per tonne for imports of 150,000 tonnes of maize into Portugal , the full quota set aside for that destination , European grain officials said . 
the preferential imports come under a long standing agreement to compensate the U.S. and other maize exporters for losses after Portugal joined the European Union . 
the maize is normally imported from the United States .
