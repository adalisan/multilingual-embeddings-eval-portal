contracts on the treasury bill repurchase market in Shanghai closed mostly down on Friday with institutions reducing their business on market uncertainties ahead of the weekend , traders said . 
the most active three-day contract plunged to 2.59 percent from 13.50 percent on Thursday . 
total turnover fell to 3.58 million lots from 5.99 million . 
one lot is 1,000 yuan face value of treasury bills . 
traders said institutions believed that there would be a major technical rebound on the domestic A share markets next week and that this dampend sentiment on the repo market .
