Taiwan &apos;s Nationalist Party-owned business said on Tuesday it plans to invest US $ 18 million in the property market and computer software in the United States . 
Central Investment Holding Co , one of the ruling party &apos;s major investment arms , plans to set up a joint-venture fund with U.S. firm Trinity Investment L.L.C. 
to jointly invest in Hawaii &apos;s properties and hotels , the party &apos;s Business Management Committee said in a statement . 
Central Investment also plans to invest US $ 3 million in U.S. software firm ENVIVE due to the potential growth of the firm &apos;s Inspector software , it said .
