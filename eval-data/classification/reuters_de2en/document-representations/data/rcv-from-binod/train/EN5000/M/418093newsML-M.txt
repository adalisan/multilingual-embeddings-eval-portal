Nebraska feedlot managers passed bids of $ 112.00 per cwt on a dressed basis and $ 69.00 on a live basis Tuesday , market sources said . 
Packer inquiry was light and no trading was reported early Tuesday , they said . 
Feedlots were pricing cattle from $ 114.00 to $ 115.00 on a dressed basis and mostly $ 71.00 on a live basis , with a few offers of $ 72.00 .
