Philippines central bank governor Gabriel Singson said on Thursday that he projected a US $ 13-14 billion trade deficit in 1997 following a widening of the trade deficit to US $ 11.2 billion in 1996 . 
in a speech in Hong Kong , Singson also said he projected a 1997 current account deficit of US $ 4.1 billion , equivalent to 4.2 percent of gross national product .
