the Australian wool market eastern indicator rose four cents to 633 cents a kg clean on Wednesday at sales in Sydney and Adelaide , wool International said . 
Merino fleece was mixed , with 19 / 21 / 22 microns one percent dearer , 20m 1.5 percent dearer , 23 / 26m slightly cheaper , 24m unchanged and 25m up to one percent cheaper . 
Merino skirtings of 19m were slightly dearer , and 20 / 21m one percent cheaper . 
Crossbreds were cheaper on a limited offering , with 27 / 29m slightly cheaper , 28m unchanged , 30m up to one percent cheaper and 31m 0.5 percent cheaper . 
in cardings , lambs and locks were unchanged with crutchings 0.5 percent dearer . 
WI said trading saw general competition with strong support from China and local mills . 
of the 27,714 bales offered , 6.1 percent were passed in .
