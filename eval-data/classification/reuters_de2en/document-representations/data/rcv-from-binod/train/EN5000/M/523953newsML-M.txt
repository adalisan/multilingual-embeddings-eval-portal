0715 GMT - The Romanian leu currency opened idle on Monday at 7,040 / 100 to the dollar , on a dormant interbank market , operators said . 
* Traders said they expected leu-dollar rates to remain flat throughout the week , with the dollar supply still outrunning demand . 
* Dealers said money market prices , which opened at 80-90 percent on overnight funds , were poised to move upward as a few major players were chasing for the leu . 
* Leu-dollar retail rates lingered at some 6,800 / 6,950 .
