Shanghai Customs officers recently seized 4.6 grams of heroin in an express mail parcel being sent to Japan , the official Xinhua news agency reported . 
the agency said on Thursday the parcel was sent by a person using an assumed name and the case is still under investigation . 
it said Shanghai customs first started investigating the practice of smuggling heroin out of the country using the mail service in April last year and is stepping up efforts to stop the practice .
