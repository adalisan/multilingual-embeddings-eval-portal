the Australian wool market fell another eight cents a kg to 556 cents at sales in Sydney and Geelong , wool International ( WI ) said . 
in merinos , 19 micron was 5.0 percent cheaper , while 20 mm was three percent cheaper and 21 mm was two percent cheaper . 
Merino skirtings of 19 and 21 mm were two percent cheaper and 20 mm was three percent cheaper . 
Crossbreds were cheaper , with the exception of 31 mm which was unchanged . 
WI said most interest came from Japan and Western Europe with support from other sectors . 
sales continue on Thursday in Sydney and Geelong , with about 25,400 bales on offer , WI said .
