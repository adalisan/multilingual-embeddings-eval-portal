the central bank of Lithuania said on Tuesday its official reserves dipped to $ 835.9 million in September from $ 852.5 million the previous month , the first fall for several months . 
the reserves had been steadily gaining as Lithuania recovered from a banking crisis at the end of last year and hit a high point in August . 
the central bank has to keep enough foreign currency reserves to back the litas currency at four to one dollar .
