the Italian Treasury said it will issue three , five and 10-year fixed rate bonds ( BTP ) and seven year floating rate certificates ( CCT ) at its regular mid-month auction .

* 1.5 trln lire 11th tranche 1 / 10 / 96 3-yr BTPs paying a 7.50 percent coupon . 
* 1.5 trln lire 11th tranche 15 / 09 / 96 5-yr BTPs paying 7.75 percent coupon . 
* 1.5 trln lire 5th tranche 1 / 11 / 96 7-yr CCTs paying an initial 3.80 PCT coupon .
