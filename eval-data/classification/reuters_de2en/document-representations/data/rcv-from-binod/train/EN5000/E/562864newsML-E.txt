China paid 3.16 billion yuan ( $ 380 million ) in tax rebates to its exporters in March , the China Securities said on Tuesday . 
tax authorities at all levels had taken measures to speed up paying out the rebates and payment work for April had gone well , the newspaper said but gave no further details . 
Chinese exporters were paid 82.63 billion yuan in tax rebates last year , up 50.5 percent from 1995 , offical figures show . 
China moved last year to speed up paying delayed tax rebates to exporters to try to stimulate slumping exports . 
( $ 1 = 8.3 yuan )
