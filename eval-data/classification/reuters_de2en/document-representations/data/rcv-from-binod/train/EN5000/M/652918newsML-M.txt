Colombia &apos;s peso gained 80 centavos to close at 1,079.8 against the dollar in stable trading , dealers said . 
dealers said banks were looking to build up dollar positions early in the session although two large banks moved to sell greenbacks around midday . 
there was also an influx of petrodollars and coffee earnings into the market . 
dealers reported 379 trades worth a total $ 186 million . 
the peso opened at 1,081.5 , and crested at 1,079.5 . 
the intraday low was 1,082 .
