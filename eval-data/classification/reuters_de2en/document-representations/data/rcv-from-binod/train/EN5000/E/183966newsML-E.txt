the rise in the year-on-year rate of French inflation in October was mainly due to higher oil prices and an increase in car prices as a government scheme to encourage car buying ended , the Treasury said . 
national statistics institute INSEE said earlier on Tuesday French consumer prices rose 0.2-0.3 percent in October from September and climbed 1.7-1.8 percent compared with the same month a year earlier . 
prices rose 1.6 in September compared with the previous year . 
the Treasury said excluding energy prices the year-on-year increase was 1.3 percent . 
the cost of petrol products in France rose 2.4 percent in the month and climbed 10.8 percent year on year . 
the Treasury also said the monthly rise in prices was affected by two seasonal factors moving in opposite directions . 
the cost of fresh food rose while the cost of some rail and train fares fell .
