the Belgian central bank released the following indices based on its monthly survey of three key economic sectors - 
overall - 7.7 -12.1 
manufacturing -10.8 -14.5 
trade + 3.8 - 6.3 
building - 5.3 - 7.0 
note - The leading index is based on central bank surveys of manufacturing , construction and retailing and calculated on the basis of the difference between positive and negative answers to the bank &apos;s questions . 
it is slightly positive at the top of an economic cycle , while it is strongly negative at the depth of a recession . 
note - In the statement the central bank said the strong rise in the July indicator confirmed the upward trend seen since the beginning of the year . 
there were improvements in January and February before a fall in March , but since then the index has risen . 
the central bank said there were improvements in all sectors . 
it warned the increases should be treated with caution in view of the seasonally lower number of responses in the survey .
