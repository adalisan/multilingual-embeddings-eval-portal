Morocco &apos;s Bank al-Maghrib ( central bank ) withdrew 600 million dirhams from the market at its weekly repurchase auction , a bank official said on Thursday . 
the bank received bids totalling 3.580 billion dirhams and allocated 2.700 billion dirhams at a 6.5 percent interest rate . 
it satisfied 75.42 percent of demand for liquidity , the bank said . 
the amount allocated was less than the 3.3 billion dirhams leaving the market on Wednesday . 
the next auction will take place on December 25 , 1996 .
