Algeria &apos;s state oil and gas company Sonatrach has cut its official posted prices for liquefied petroleum gas ( LPG ) for April , Sonatrach said on Monday . 
propane prices have been set at $ 175 a tonne for loading at Bethioua , a drop of $ 20 from the official price for March . 
butane falls to $ 180 a tonne , a drop of $ 35 from last month . 
the premium for loadings from Skikda is $ 7 a tonne , Sonatrach said .
