Sri Lanka &apos;s state-owned Ceylon Petroleum Corp ( CPC ) has issued a tender to buy gas oil ( diesel ) and jet fuel for Oct 26-27 delivery on a cost-and-freight ( c-and-f ) basis to Colombo , a CPC official said on Wednesday . 
the tender seeks 120,000 barrels of 1.0-percent sulphur gas oil and 80,000 barrels of aviation jet fuel . 
the tender closes on October 8 and offers will be valid for two days . 
in its last tender , CPC bought 160,000 barrels of gas oil and 40,000 barrels of jet fuel from Swiss trader Glencore at 89 cents a barrel over Singapore spot quotes for October 11-12 delivery , c-and-f Colombo .
