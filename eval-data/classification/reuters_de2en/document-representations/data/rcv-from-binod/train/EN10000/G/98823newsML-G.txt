at least 16 people died and 50 were taken to hospital in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu after drinking illicit liquor , the Press Trust of India ( PTI ) reported on Sunday . 
quoting local authorities , PTI said 68 people drank the liquor on Saturday night in the Pudukottai district , 350 km ( 220 miles ) from the Madras . 
police have arrested the owners of the liquor shop . 
hundreds of people , mostly poor , die every year in India after consuming cheap illicit liquor .
