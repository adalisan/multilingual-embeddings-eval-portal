ASW Holdings plc said on Thursday that it is seeking a buyer for its UK metal recycling businesses in order to reduce debt . 
the wires and nails manufacturing group said in a statement that its metal recycling businesses had a total turnover of 75 million stg in the year ended December 31 , 1995 . 
it added that the businesses have been consistently profitable in the last three years . 
ASW said a substantial reduction of debt will be achieved as a result of this initiative and the intended sale of non-core businesses announced earlier this year .
