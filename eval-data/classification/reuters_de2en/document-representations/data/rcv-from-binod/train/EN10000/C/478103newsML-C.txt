Embotelladora Andina SA on Tuesday filed a shelf registration with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission to sell up to $ 500 million in debt securities . 
the company , known in English as Andina bottling Co . , said its net proceeds will be used to repay certain short-term debts , to finance the acquistion of additonal Coca-Cola bottlers in South America and other strategic investments and for general corporate purposes . 
the company , based in Santiago , Chile , is the largest producer of soft drinks in Chile and one of the largest soft drink producers in Brazil and Argentina .
