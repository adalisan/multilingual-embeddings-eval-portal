consumer prices in Ivory Coast rose by 1.5 percent in June from May , giving a year-on-year inflation rate of 5.9 percent , up from 5.0 percent in May , data from the National Statistics Institute ( ins ) showed . 
this benchmark rate is calculated from separate indices for blue-collar and white-collar workers , accounting respectively for 80 percent and 20 percent of the so-called &quot; combined index &quot; . 
the ins did not provide any further information on the combined index . 
the government has forecast an inflation rate of 5.0 percent for 1997 .
