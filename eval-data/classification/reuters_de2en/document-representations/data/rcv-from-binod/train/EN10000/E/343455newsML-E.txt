South African retail trade sales rose a seasonally adjusted 3.9 percent month-on-month in November to 11.88 billion rand , latest Central Statistical Service ( CSS ) data shows . 
year-on-year , seasonally adjusted retail sales were up 12.8 percent from 10.53 billion rand in November 1995 , the CSS said . 
on an actual basis , retail trade sales were up 8.5 percent month-on-month and up 13 percent year-on-year .
