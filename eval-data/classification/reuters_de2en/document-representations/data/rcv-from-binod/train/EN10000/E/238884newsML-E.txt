China expects to draw $ 49.5 billion worth of actual direct foreign investment in 1997 , up by 15 percent compared with 1996 , the China Daily said on Thursday . 
in the first half of this year , China attracted overseas capital of $ 19.64 billion , rising 20 percent compared with the year-ago period , the newspaper said , quoting a report by the State Information Centre . 
the report forecast that direct investment next year would maintain its steady growth although the investment structure would change , with more investment into the more remote and under-developed central and western regions . 
in the first seven months , investment in central regions grew by 38.3 percent while investment in western areas rose by 55.6 percent , it said .
