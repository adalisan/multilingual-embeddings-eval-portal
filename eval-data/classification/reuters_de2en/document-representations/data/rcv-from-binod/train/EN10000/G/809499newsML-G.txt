Turkish police seized 170 kg ( 374 pounds ) of heroin in the southeastern province of Van and arrested two men , one a non-commissioned army officer , state-run Anatolian news agency said on Tuesday . 
it said the drugs were found in secret compartments of a car used by the two men during a search carried out late on Monday . 
police said no further information was available as the investigation was continuing , the agency said . 
Turkey lies on the Balkan route of the drugs trade used to supply the West with drugs from Asia and the Middle East .
