President Boris Yeltsin &apos;s heart surgery began in Moscow early on Tuesday , Itar-Tass news agency said . 
before the operation , he signed a decree handing over his presidential powers , including control of Russia &apos;s nuclear arsenal , to Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin , TASS said . 
if all goes according to plan , he will resume his authority once he emerges from the anaesthetic and is fit to govern . 
Yeltsin , 65 , was driven in a motorcade from the Barvikha sanatorium west of Russian capital to the Moscow Cardiology Centre where the triple or quadruple bypass was being carried out . 
the operation was to be performed by a team headed by Russian surgeon Renat Akchurin , advised by U.S. heart surgery pioneer Michael DeBakey and others .
