Kazakhstan posted a foreign trade surplus of $ 183.2 million in January , against a positive trade balance of $ 208.0 million in the same month last year , the National Statistics Committee said on Wednesday . 
it gave the following data for Kazakhstan trade figures in millions of dollars : 
exports 318.0 584.8 
imports 134.8 376.8 
balance + 183.2 + 208.0 
the committee said that exports of mineral resources from the oil-rich Central Asian state accounted for 41 percent of all exports , followed by metal products , which made up 28 percent of exports . 
note : the table does not include Kazakhstan &apos;s trade with countries of the Commonwealth of Independent States .
