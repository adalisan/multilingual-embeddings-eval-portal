a ferry carrying 60 people sank in the Nile in southern Egypt on Thursday and security sources said many were feared drowned . 
the ferry , which was carrying a number of people returning home from a funeral , was in collision with a barge . 
six people swam ashore and others may also have done so , but it was not immediately possible to say because the accident happened after dark , the sources said . 
no bodies had been found , they added . 
the accident happened near the village of Atnidan in Minya province about 260 km ( 160 miles ) south of Cairo at a point where the Nile is several hundred metres ( yards ) wide .
