European Parliament President Klaus Haensch will make an official visit to Israel and the Palestine authority from November 17 to 21 . 
Haensch will address both the Knesset ( parliament ) and the Palestine Legislative Council ( PLC ) . 
in Israel , Haensch will meet Israeli President Ezer Weizman , Speaker of the Knesset Dan Tichon , Foreign Minister David Levy and Labour Party chief Shimon Peres . 
during his visit to the Palestine authority , he will hold talks with Palestinian leader Yasser Arafat , Speaker of the PLC Ahmed Qurei , Health Minister Riad El Zaanuun and Elias Freij , the Mayor of Bethlehem .
