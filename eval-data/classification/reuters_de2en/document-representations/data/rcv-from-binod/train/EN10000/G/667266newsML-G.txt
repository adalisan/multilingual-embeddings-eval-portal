typhoon Opal is becoming an increasing threat to shipping in the western Pacific as it tracks towards Japan , independent forecaster weather Services Corp ( WSC ) said on Tuesday . 
Opal has winds of 100 mph and is located east of the northern Philippines and about 600 miles south-southeast of Okinawa . 
it is expected to have winds up to 130 mph in 24-48 hours as it tracks east of Okinawa and towards Shikoku , Japan . 
it is expected to reach Shikoku in about 60 hours with winds of 115-120 mph . 
should this track hold , the island would experience very heavy rains leading to flooding and possible mudslides , WSC added .
