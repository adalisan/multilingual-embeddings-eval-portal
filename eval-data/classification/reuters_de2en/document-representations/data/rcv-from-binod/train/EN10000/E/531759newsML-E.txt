Argentine industrial production in March rose 8.0 percent on the same month last year , the Economy Ministry announced Wednesday . 
in the first quarter , output was up 7.2 percent compared with the same period a year earlier . 
industrial production rose 12.2 percent from the previous month in non-seasonally adjusted terms and fell 0.8 percent when seasonally-adjusted . 
earlier Wednesday , the FIEL private consultancy said March industrial production rose 5.8 percent from a year earlier , according to preliminary figures . 
the average estimate for March industrial production by analysts surveyed by Reuters was for a 7.2 percent increase .
