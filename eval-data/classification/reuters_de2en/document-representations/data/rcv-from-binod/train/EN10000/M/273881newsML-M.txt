the Italian Treasury said it will issue a total of 23 trillion lire of medium to long-term paper for sale at its regular end-month auction . 
the Treasury said in a statement said it would offer : 
* 5.5 trillion lire of three-year fixed rate bonds ( BTP ) paying a 6.0 percent coupon . 
* 5.0 trillion lire of five-year 6.25 percent BTPs 
* 4.0 trillion lire of 10-year 7.75 percent BTPs . 
* 8.5 trillion lire of 7-year floating rate certificates ( CCTs ) paying a first coupon of 3.8 percent .
