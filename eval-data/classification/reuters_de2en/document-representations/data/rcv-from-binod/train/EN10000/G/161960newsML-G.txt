more than 40 percent of the French are exasperated by endless violence by Corsican separatists and ready to grant the turbulent Mediterranean island independence , an opinion poll showed on Saturday . 
the poll , by the BVA group for Canal Plus television , said 42 percent would vote for Corsica to be independent , 30 percent would vote against , while 15 percent would cast blank ballots and 13 percent had no answer . 
the poll was conducted from October 24-26 among 990 people throughout France 
a poll conducted last month solely in Corsica showed 91 percent of people there opposed independence , seven percent were in favour and two percent had no opinion . 
separatist movements have conducted a 20-year guerrilla campaign against central rule by authorities in Paris .
