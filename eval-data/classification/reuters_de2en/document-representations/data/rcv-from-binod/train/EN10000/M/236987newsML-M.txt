Singapore physical jet fuel was sold late on Thursday at a new five-year high of $ 34.20 per barrel on a free-on-board ( fob ) basis by a US major to a Dutch trader , the seller confirmed . 
the previous five-year peak was traded in January at around $ 33.75 per barrel . 
traders atributed the recent surge in jet fuel prices to large purchases by Japan and South Korea for the current cold weather and from Indonesia ahead of the Moslem fasting month in January . 
the Dutch trader had also bought jet fuel about two weeks ago at a post-winter high of $ 31.95 a barrel for end-November lifting fob Singapore .
