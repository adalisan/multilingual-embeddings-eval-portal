Bangladeshi tea prices were easier at the weekly auction on Tuesday , brokers said . 
prices of most grades fell marginally on lower demand , forcing traders to withdraw over 39 percent of their offers . 
buyers for Poland , Pakistan , Afghanistan and Russia participated in the bidding . 
more than 710,892 million kg of teas were sold at the auction . 
the average tea price was 43.25 taka , down 0.20 from the previous sale . 
the next auction will be held on September 24 when 1.42 million kg of teas will be on offer .
