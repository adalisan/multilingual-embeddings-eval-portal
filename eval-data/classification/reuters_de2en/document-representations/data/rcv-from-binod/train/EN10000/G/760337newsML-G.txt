two people were killed and three injured in a powerful bomb blast in the northern Indian state of Punjab on Monday , the Press Trust of India ( PTI ) said . 
the news agency said the blast occurred at Dhandari near the city of Ludhiana in the state which has seen more than a decade of Sikh separatist violence since the early 1980s . 
the blast occurred in a container depot where a consignment of imported iron scrap was being unloaded from a container , PTI quoted a senior police official as saying . 
it did not give details .
