Irish industrial production rose a seasonally adjusted 11.7 percent in January after a 6.7 percent drop in December , Central Statistics Office ( CSO ) data showed on Tuesday . 
the CSO said the January unadjusted index number for all industries was 269.3 , a 13.6 percent rise over January 1996 . 
production in the manufacturing goods industries was up 13.0 percent on the month and 14.1 percent year-on-year while transportable goods output rose 13.1 percent in January for a year-on-year increase of 13.9 percent .
