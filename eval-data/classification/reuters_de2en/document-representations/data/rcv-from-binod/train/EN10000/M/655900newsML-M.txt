South African wool prices were mixed at the last weekly auction , the wool Board said . 
it said 12,999 bales were offered at this week &apos;s sale , of which 97.1 percent was sold . 
price quotes for long Merino fleeces of 20 micron fell 0.2 percent , 21 micron rose 2.2 percent , 22 micron fell 0.2 percent , 23 micron rose 3.0 percent , 24 micron rose 2.3 percent and 25 micron rose 2.3 percent . 
prices for Merino bellies rose 1.1 percent , Merino lambs fell 2.7 percent and Merino locks fell 1.2 percent . 
the offering included 1,800 bales of other White Wools , 1,110 bales of Ciskei / Transkei , 2,127 bales of Lesotho and 1,190 bales of coarse and coloured . 
of the 459 bales of 20 micron offered , 95.4 percent was sold . 
of the 1,272 bales of 21 micron offered , 97.3 percent was sold . 
of the 1,791 bales of 22 micron offered , 96.8 percent was sold . 
of the 1,363 bales of 23 micron offered , 99.2 percent was sold . 
of the 694 bales of 24 micron offered , 98.6 percent was sold and of the 268 bales of 25 micron offered , 98.1 percent was sold . 
the next sale will take place in August , when about 10,000 bales will be on offer .
