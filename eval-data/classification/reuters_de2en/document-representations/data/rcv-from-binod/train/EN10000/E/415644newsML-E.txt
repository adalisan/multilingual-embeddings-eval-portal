British M0 money supply rose to a provisional seasonally adjusted 24.933 billion sterling in the week to February 26 from 24.913 billion the previous week , the Bank of England said on Monday . 
on an unadjusted basis M0 rose to 24.285 billion sterling from 24.179 billion the week before . 
notes and coins in circulation dropped to 24.818 billion sterling from 24.880 billion on a seasonally adjusted basis but rose to an unadjusted 24.170 billion from 24.146 the previous week .
