the Italian city of Trieste said its March consumer price index stood at 0.1 percent month-on-month and 2.2 percent year-on-year . 
in February , Trieste &apos;s inflation rate was 0.3 percent and 2.4 percent respectively . 
the general index and housing and utility charges sector have been revised to take account of a court ruling last month to suspend electricity tarriff cuts . 
Trieste is one of 11 Italian cities publishing inflation data on Thursday and Friday . 
the city &apos;s CPI has a weighting of 2.49 percent on the national index . 
the 11 sample cities have a combined weighting of 75.66 percent on the total index .
