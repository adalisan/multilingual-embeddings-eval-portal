Christmas Day in the Holy Land began with a jolt on Wednesday when Israelis called radio stations to say their homes had been shaken by what appeared to be a tremor . 
there were no reports of injuries . 
Israeli army radio said residents began calling shortly after midnight ( 2200 GMT Tuesday ) from areas throughout the country to say the furniture in their homes had been shaken for several seconds . 
Israel Radio said it received similar phone calls . 
the Israeli Seismological Institute was checking the reports . 
earthquakes are not all that uncommon in Israel and the rest of the eastern Mediterranean . 
whatever rocked other parts of the Holy Land did not shake Bethlehem , birthplace of Jesus Christ , where a midnight mass was taking place , witnesses said .
