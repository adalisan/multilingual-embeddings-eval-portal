Sri Lanka &apos;s March inflation , based on a 12-month moving average , remained unchanged at 16.7 percent , compared with February , the census and Statistics Department said on Monday . 
the Colombo Consumers &apos; Price Index ( CCPI ) for March fell 21.7 points or 1.1 percent to 2,009.6 from 2,031.3 in February , it said in a statement . 
the CCPI dropped mainly because of falls in the prices of rice , sugar , dried chillies , red onions , coconuts , coconut oil , potatoes , and some varieties of vegetables and fish , it said . 
but it said prices of coriander , cumin seed , limes , beef , mutton , eggs and some varieties of dried fish rose during the month .
