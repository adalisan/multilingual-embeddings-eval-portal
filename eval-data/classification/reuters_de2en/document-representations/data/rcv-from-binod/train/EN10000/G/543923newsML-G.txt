Saudi Arabia on Monday beheaded a Saudi man for smuggling hashish into the kingdom , the Saudi Interior Ministry said . 
the beheading raised to 21 the number of people executed in the conservative Islamic kingdom so far this year . 
an official statement carried by the Saudi news agency said Hamad bin Mohammad Al-Dowsari was executed in the al-Ehsa area in the Eastern Province after being found guilty of smuggling an unspecified amount of drugs into Saudi Arabia .
