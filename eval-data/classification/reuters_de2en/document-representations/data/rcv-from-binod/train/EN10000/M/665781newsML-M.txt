the National Market Exchanges - comprised of the Boston , Chicago , Pacific and Philadelphia stock exchanges - said on Monday total equity trades rose 12 percent in May versus the same period last year . 
equity trades totalled 1.31 million transactions , while combined volume at the exchanges for the month rose six percent to 897.58 million , the National Market Exchanges said . 
the National Market System was created in 1975 , to link all U.S. stock exchanges in order to enhance competition . 
through the Intermarket Trading System , members of any exchange can access any other exchange displaying a better price on any particular stock , it said .
