the Central Reserve Bank of Peru said Wednesday it accepted bids for 56-day certificates of Deposit ( CDs ) worth 20 million soles at an effective average annual rate of 15.45 percent during a bank auction BCQZ . 
the CDs have a January 16 starting date . 
the accepted bids also had a minimum rate of 15.30 percent and a maximum rate of 15.50 percent , the bank said . 
at the last CD auction Thursday , 28-day CDs drew an average rate of 14.55 PCT . 
at Wednesday &apos;s auction , the bank said it received bids on the CDs totalling 53.50 million soles . 
the bank had offered 20 million soles worth of CDs . 
the average effective annual interest rate for the certificates is used as an informal indicator for interest rates in the Peruvian financial system .
