the Tokyo commodity Exchange ( TOCOM ) said on Tuesday that it would raise the margin requirement on its November rubber futures contract by 40,000 yen per trading lot , effective from Wednesday . 
the increase will bring the total November margin requirement to 102,500 yen , TOCOM said in a statement . 
the increase is aimed at cooling overheated trade . 
the requirement on the spot October contract will be unchanged at 157,750 yen , while those on other contracts will remain at 52,500 yen .
