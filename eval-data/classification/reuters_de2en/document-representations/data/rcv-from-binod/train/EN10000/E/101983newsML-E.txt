London , October 8 ( LPC ) - The Hungarian Bank for Investment and Development has invited banks to bid to arrange a $ 60 million syndicated loan for general corporate financing , according to Janos Albert , director of the Budapest-based bank . 
bids are expected to be in hand at the borrower within several days , according to Albert . 
the bank plans to use the credit to fund further lending . 
in August , the Hungarian Bank for Investment and Development signed a $ 75 million term loan at 80 bps with a 3.5-year maturity . 
that loan was arranged by ABN AMRO Bank , Bayerische Vereinsbank and Central-European Investment Bank and was increased from $ 50 million after an oversubscription .
