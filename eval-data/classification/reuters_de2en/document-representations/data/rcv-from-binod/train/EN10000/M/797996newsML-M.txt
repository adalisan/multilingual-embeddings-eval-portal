Vietnam sold 150 billion Dong ( $ 12.83 million ) worth of one-year Treasury notes with an annual coupon of 11.0 percent to domestic institutions at an auction on Wednesday , an official at the State Bank of Vietnam said . 
at the last sale of government paper , on August 6 , the central bank sold 60 billion Dong worth of one-year notes at a rate of 11.2 percent . 
the issue date of the notes sold on Wednesday is August 15 and maturity will be on August 15 , 1998 . 
the bank said allotments of stock , in Dong , were as follows : 
industrial and Commercial Bank of Vietnam 120.5 billion 
Vietnam General Insurance Corporation ( Bao Viet ) 27.0 billion 
Bank for Investment and Development of Vietnam 2.0 billion 
national Reinsurance Company 0.5 billion
