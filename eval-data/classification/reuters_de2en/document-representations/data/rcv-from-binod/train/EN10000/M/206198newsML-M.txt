South African wool prices were mixed at the latest weekly auction , the wool Board said . 
it said 11,375 bales were offered at this week &apos;s sale , of which 98.9 percent was sold . 
price quotes for long Merino fleeces of 20 micron fell 0.2 percent , 21 micron rose 1.3 percent , 22 micron rose 1.3 percent , 23 micron rose 0.1 percent , 24 micron rose 0.6 percent and 25 micron remain unchanged . 
prices for Merino bellies fell 0.3 percent , Merino lambs fell 2.4 percent and Merino locks rose 1.4 percent . 
the offering included 2,338 bales of other White Wools , 161 bales of Ciskei / Transkei , 277 bales of Lesotho and 243 bales of coarse and coloured . 
of the 442 bales of 20 micron offered , 97.7 percent was sold . 
of the 1,191 bales of 21 micron offered , 98.9 percent was sold . 
of 1,876 bales of 22 micron offered , 98.9 percent was sold , Of 1,375 bales of 23 micron offered , 99.7 percent was sold . 
all 24 and 25 micron bales on offer were sold . 
the next sale will take place on 27 November , when 10,000 bales will be on offer .
