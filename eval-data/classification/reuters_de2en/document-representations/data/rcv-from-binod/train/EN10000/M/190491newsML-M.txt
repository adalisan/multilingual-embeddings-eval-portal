foreigners were net sellers of Japanese stocks on the Tokyo , Osaka and Nagoya stock exchanges in the week of November 5 to November 8 , exchange data showed . 
foreigners have been net sellers for the past five weeks after buying continuously for the previous five weeks , according to the data . 
the figures , which are rounded , are as follows : 
total foreign buying 293.53 billion yen 
total foreign selling 360.74 billion yen 
net selling 67.22 billion yen 
previous week net selling 87.46 billion yen
