a South African trade delegation will visit three countries in the Middle East , including Iran , in October to foster stronger ties with the region , the Department of Trade and Industry said on Thursday . 
the delegation will be led by deputy trade minister Phumzile Mlambo-Ngcuka and will visit Iran , the United Arab Emirates and Saudi Arabia . 
&quot; similiar to earlier initiatives in other parts of the world , this mission aims to further trade relations with the Middle East region , &quot; the department said . 
the visit to Iran will coincide with the participation of South Africa in the 22nd Iran International Trade Fair taking place in Tehran from October 2 .
