Greece &apos;s industrial production and manufacturing output rose a provisional 1.4 percent year-on-year and 0.3 percent , respectively , in June after rising 3.2 percent and 2.3 percent year-on-year in May , the National Statistics Service ( NSS ) said . 
the increase in June industrial production was mainly due to a rise of 11.6 percent year-on-year in mining output and 2.3 percent in electricity-gaslight , it said .
