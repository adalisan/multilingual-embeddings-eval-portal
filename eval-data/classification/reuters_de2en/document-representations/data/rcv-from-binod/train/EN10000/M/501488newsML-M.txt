the Osaka Securities Exchange ( OSE ) said on Thursday that it will begin spread transactions for Nikkei 225 futures on May 16 . 
with the start of the transactions , the OSE said it would extend the trading hours for Nikkei 225 futures and options by 10 minutes , to 0610 GMT . 
the bourse began spread trades of Nikkei 300 futures in April of last year .
