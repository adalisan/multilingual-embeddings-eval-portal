at least nine people died when an explosion ripped through a barge carrying 60 tonnes of gasoline on a river in the southern Chinese province of Guangdong , the Wenhui Bao newspaper reported on Tuesday . 
the paper said the wooden barge exploded and burst into flames in the early hours of Sunday while at a pier in the city of Leizhou , 550 km ( 340 miles ) southwest of Hong Kong . 
it said the bodies of nine crew members had been recovered while several were still missing . 
the cause of the explosion was being investigated , but the barge was carrying gasoline without permission , the newspaper said . 
it gave no further details .
