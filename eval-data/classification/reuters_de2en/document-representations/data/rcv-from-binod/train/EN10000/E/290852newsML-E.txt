Brazilian exports in December totalled $ 3.7 billion , with an average daily amount of $ 189 million , a local television said on Tuesday . 
TV Globo said December figures took 1996 exports to total $ 47.7 billion , with a rise of a slight 2.7 percent over 1995 . 
official export figures for December will be revealed today by the Ministry of Industry and Commerce at 1430 local / 1630 GMT .
