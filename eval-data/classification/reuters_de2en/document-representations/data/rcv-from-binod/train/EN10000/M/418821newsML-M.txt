the Central Bank of Cyprus will auction up to 15 million pounds ( $ 29.5 million ) in 91-day treasury bills with a March 12 issue date , the bank said on Tuesday . 
bids , which can be competitive or non-competitive , can be made from March 11 to March 12 at 9.00 a.m. ( 0700 GMT ) . 
competitive bids will be accepted in part or in whole if the price is equal or higher than that set by the central bank . 
those making non-competitive bids will pay the average price of all bids submitted and accepted by the central bank .
