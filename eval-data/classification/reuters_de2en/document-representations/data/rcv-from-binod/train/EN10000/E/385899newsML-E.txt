Portuguese non-tradable goods prices rose 5.1 percent year-on-year in January against 5.0 percent in December , the National Statistics Institute ( INE ) said on Tuesday . 
it attributed the increase to adjustments in electricity and fuel prices . 
tradable goods prices rose 1.8 percent , unchanged on the previous month . 
it said factory gate prices for manufactured goods other than food and petrol derivatives rose 0.8 percent year-on-year in the three months to November , similar to the change recorded in the third quarter of 1996 .
