Polish consumer prices rose 1.3 percent in November against a 1.4 percent rise in October bringing year-on-year inflation down to 19.1 percent , the Central Statistical Office ( Gus ) said . 
CPI rose 17.1 percent in the January-November period , compared with a 19.8 percent rise in the corresponding period last year .
