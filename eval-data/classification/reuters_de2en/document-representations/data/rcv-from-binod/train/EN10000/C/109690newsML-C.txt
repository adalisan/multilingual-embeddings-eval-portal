LG metals Corp , a unit of South Korea &apos;s LG Group , said on Thursday its copper production rose 5.6 percent to 183,413 tonnes in the first nine months of this year against 173,610 tonnes a year earlier . 
an LG metals official said the company produced 51.3 tonnes of gold in the nine months , up 49.6 percent from 34.3 tonnes a year ago , and 159 tonnes of silver , down 17.1 percent from 191.7 tonnes . 
in 1995 , LG metals &apos;s output totalled 233,300 tonnes of copper , 40.69 tonnes of gold and 232 tonnes of silver .
