Taiwan &apos;s state-owned Chinese Petroleum Corp ( CPC ) has bought by tender 7,000 tonnes of methyl tertiary butyl ether ( MTBE ) for April 6-20 at $ 280- $ 283 per tonne cost-and-freight Taiwan , from Saudi Basic Industries Corp ( SABIC ) , industry sources said on Wednesday . 
a CPC official told Reuters around five offers were received for the tender which closed March 3 and sought the cargo for delivery to Kaohsiung and Keelung . 
the official said the prices were reasonable , and lower than CPC &apos;s last tender for March 1-15 delivery at $ 283 c-and-f .
