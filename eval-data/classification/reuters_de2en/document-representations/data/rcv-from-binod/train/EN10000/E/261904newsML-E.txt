the Belgian finance ministry said on Tuesday an issue of state notes had raised a total 6.789 billion Belgian francs by the end of the fourth subscription day . 
the ministry said in a statement that altogether it had sold 4.809 billion francs of five year notes , extendable to seven years . 
the notes carry a fixed coupon of 4.70 percent . 
notes with revisable coupons and put options after three , five and seven years raised 1.979 billion francs , the finance ministry said .
