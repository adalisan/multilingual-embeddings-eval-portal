Palestinian President Yasser Arafat will meet U.S. Middle East peace envoy Dennis Ross in the Moroccan capital of Rabat , a senior Palestinian official said on Wednesday . 
the official , who asked not to be identified , confirmed a news report that Arafat , due in Morocco early on Thursday , would meet the special envoy dispatched by U.S. President Bill Clinton to revive Israeli-Palestinian peace talks . 
an aide to Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu said the Israeli leader would meet Ross after the envoy saw Arafat . 
the crisis emerged last week when Netanyahu broke ground on a Jewish settlement of 6,500 houses in Arab East Jerusalem . 
Palestinian protesters have clashed with Israeli forces in the West Bank for a week .
