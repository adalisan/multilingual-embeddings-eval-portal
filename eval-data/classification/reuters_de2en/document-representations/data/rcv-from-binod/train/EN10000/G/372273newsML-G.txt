authorities in the Bahamas on Tuesday returned a group of 41 Cuban boat people who had reached the neighbouring islands after leaving their country , Cuban media said . 
the boat people , most of them from the eastern province of Camaguey , left Cuba in three separate groups during December and January , the state news agency ain said . 
George Stuart , director general at the Bahamas Foreign Ministry , flew with the returning Cubans on the flight from Nassau to Havana . 
Stuart said he would be holding talks with the Cuban Foreign Ministry on Wednesday on progress implementing an accord one year ago by which the Bahamas undertook to repatriate Cuban boat people who arrived there . 
such Cubans are generally aiming to reach the United States . 
the United States also undertook in May 1995 to return Cuban boat people intercepted trying to reach U.S. territory .
