Chile &apos;s industrial production grew 2.1 percent in August compared with the same month last year , with sales growing 0.1 percent over the same period , said the National Statistics Institute . 
the figures do not include production from the copper refining industry . 
sectors with the greatest increases in output included glass manufacturing , up 26 percent ; oil and coal derivatives , up 21 percent ; and wood products , which rose 20 percent from the same month last year . 
falling production was reported in sectors including tabacco , down 20 percent ; leather goods , down 17 percent ; and clothing , down 11 percent .
