the Mauritian rupee weakened against both dollar and pound on Thursday reflecting international market trends , foreign exchange dealers said . 
the rupee was posted at a commercial mean rate of 21.2355 to the dollar from 21.21 on Wednesday and at 35.6425 to the pound from 35.5235 the previous day . 
Forex dealers said the central bank was seen selling dollars in the interbank forex market on Wednesday mid-afternoon at the interbank prevailing rate .
