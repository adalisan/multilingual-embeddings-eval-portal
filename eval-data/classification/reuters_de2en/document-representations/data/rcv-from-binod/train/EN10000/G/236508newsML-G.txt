Madagascan authorities put the northeastern coast of the Indian Ocean island on alert on Thursday as the tropical cyclone Daniela whipped up winds of up to 220 km per hour ( 140 mph ) . 
the national meteorological office said Daniela was stationary on Thursday afternoon about 650 km ( 400 miles ) off the northeastern town of Sambava , a vanilla-growing centre . 
Daniela is the fourth cyclone of the season off Madagascar .
