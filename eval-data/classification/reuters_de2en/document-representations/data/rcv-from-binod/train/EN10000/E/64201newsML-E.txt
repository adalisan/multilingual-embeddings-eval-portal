Singapore &apos;s June retail sales index ( base 1991 ) rose 3.5 percent compared to a year ago , state television news said on Thursday . 
it quoted the government &apos;s Department of Statistics as saying July sales were four percent higher than in June , the highest month-on-month rise for the year . 
total retail sales for the month were worth Singapore $ 2.0 billion , it added . 
it said the growth was partly due to the &quot; Great Singapore Sale &quot; annual price discounts in July .
