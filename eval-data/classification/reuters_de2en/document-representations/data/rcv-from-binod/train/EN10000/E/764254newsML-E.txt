German machinery and plant equipment orders grew by a real 16 percent in June compared with the same month the previous year , the German machinery and plant equipment association ( VDMA ) said on Tuesday . 
domestic orders were up 10 percent and foreign orders by 21 percent in the month compared with a year ago , it said . 
in the first half of 1997 as a whole , orders grew by a real four percent , with domestic orders rising five percent and foreign orders up four percent , it said . 
in the second quarter , real orders grew by eight percent compared with the same quarter in 1996 , with domestic orders rising by two percent and foreign orders by 13 percent .
