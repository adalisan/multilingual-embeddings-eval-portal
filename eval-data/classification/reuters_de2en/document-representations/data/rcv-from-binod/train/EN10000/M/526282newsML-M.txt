Australian financial markets face two key events this week with the Australian Industrial Relations Commission due to finally make a verdict on the &quot; living wage &quot; claim on Tuesday and the March quarter CPI due on Wednesday . 
anticipation that both outcomes will be market friendly put downward pressure on short-end yields last week , which also saw the bill curve flatten .
