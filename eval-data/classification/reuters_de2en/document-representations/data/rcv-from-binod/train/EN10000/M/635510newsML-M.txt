the Tokyo commodity Exchange ( TOCOM ) said on Tuesday it was raising the margin requirement on its June rubber futures contract by 10,000 yen per trading lot , effective from Wednesday . 
the increase will bring the total June rubber futures margin fee to 87,750 yen , while the requirements on other rubber futures will remain unchanged at 52,500 yen . 
the move is intended to cool overheated trade , TOCOM said in a statement .
