Slovenian shares eased 0.11 percent on Wednesday , dragged down by profit-taking in shopping centre operator BTC . 
traders forecast little movement in the overall market ahead of a decision by parliament on ratification of Slovenia &apos;s association agreement with the European Union . 
Parliament is expected to vote on the issue within two weeks . 
* warehouse and shopping centre operator BTC was the most active , falling 1.45 percent to 14,290 tolars on volume of 972 shares .
