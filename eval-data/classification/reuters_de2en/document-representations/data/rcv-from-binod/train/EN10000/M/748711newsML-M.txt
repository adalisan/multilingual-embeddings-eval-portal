the central bank of Ethiopia said on Wednesday it tendered treasury bills worth $ 29.8 million . 
the bills were for the 28-day , 91-day and 182-day catagories and will be issued at the end of July . 
the amount tendered were 100 million birr for 28-day bills maturing on August 27 , 1997 , 75 million birr maturing on October 29 , 1997 and 25 million birr maturing on January 1 , 1998 . 
the bank said tenders should be submitted to the National Bank not later than July 28 , adding that auction results would be announced on July 30 . 
( $ = 6.7060 Birr )
