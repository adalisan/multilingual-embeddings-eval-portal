the Australian wool market eastern indicator rose four cents to 608 cents a kg clean on Thursday at sales in Sydney and Geelong , wool International ( WI ) said . 
Merino fleece was dearer , with 19 / 24 microns one percent dearer and 20 / 21 / 22 / 23 / 25m 0.5 percent dearer . 
Merino skirtings of 19 / 20m were up to one percent dearer . 
in crossbreds , 26 / 27 / 28m were 0.5 percent dearer , and 29 / 30 / 31m up to one percent dearer , WI said . 
in cardings , lambs were fully firm , locks one percent dearer and crutchings generally unchanged , it said . 
WI said Japan , Western Europe and China were the principal buyers with support from most sectors of the trade . 
of the 14,622 bales offered , 5.4 percent were passed in . 
sales continue next week in Sydney , Launceston and Fremantle with about 77,500 bales to be offered .
