Munawir Sjadzali , a former Minister of Religion , has been elected chairman of the National Commission on Human Rights for the period 1996-8 . 
the election comes after the death in June of the commission &apos;s founding head , Ali Said . 
foreign Minister Ali Alatas told the United Nations that Indonesia does not accept unilateral and arbitary actions by industrialised countries which could undermine multilateral bodies such as the World Trade Organisation ( WTO ) . 
Alatas said that the upcoming WTO meeting in Singapore in December should not be linked to human rights , as some countries have wanted . 
officials of the Indonesian Embassy in The Hague have not yet been able to meet the Garuda pilot being held by Dutch police under suspicion of trafficking around 8,000 pills of the designer drug Ecstasy . 
Sampoerna International , a Singapore-based subsidiary of Indonesian tobacco manufacturer PT hm Sampoerna , has confirmed it bought a 2.85 percent stake in car manufacturer Astra International . 
this makes Putera Sampoerna the largest shareholder in the company after it was made public that he had recently bought a 9.81 percent stake in recent trading . 
Johanes B. Kotjo , who is set to take over the assets and debt of liquidated Bank Summa , said he will use the bank &apos;s capital to build a Jakarta International Trade Centre . 
the bank &apos;s assets are estimated at around 600 billion rupiah .
