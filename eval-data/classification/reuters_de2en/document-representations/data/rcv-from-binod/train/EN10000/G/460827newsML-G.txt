a Vietnamese woman committed suicide last week by setting herself on fire after quarrelling with her drunken husband , an official in Ho Chi Minh City said on Friday . 
the 37-year-old woman , whose name was given as Thanh , was taken to hospital on March 15 after pouring kerosene on her body and setting it alight , but died of her injuries the next day . 
the official said the husband and wife had argued many times in the past over his habit of staying out late and drinking .
