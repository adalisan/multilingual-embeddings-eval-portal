Israeli industrial production growth slowed last year to 5.5 percent after rising 8.5 percent in 1995 , the Central Bureau of Statistics said on Wednesday . 
it said the main area of growth was in electronic components and jewellery but food and beverage production was static and the manufacture of footware and clothing declined . 
releasing the annual figure , the bureau gave no statistics for December but said industrial production rose 0.4 percent in November compared with increases of 0.3 percent in October and 0.5 percent in November 1995 . 
earlier on Wednesday , Bank Leumi said in a report that Israel &apos;s gross domestic product would expand 3.9 percent this year , slowing from an estimated 4.4 percent in 1996 .
