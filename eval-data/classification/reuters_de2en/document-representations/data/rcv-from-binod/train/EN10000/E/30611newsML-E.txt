Argentina has launched two 375 million mark callable Eurobonds with seven- and 20-year maturities , lead manager CSFB-Effectenbank said on Tuesday . 
a nine percent seven-year offering , reoffered at 99.75 , is callable at par after five years . 
a 12 percent 20-year offering , also reoffered at 99.75 , is callable at par on the coupon dates in 2006 and 2011 .
