Chinese officials have smashed an illegal salt manufacturing and trading operation in the southern Guangdong city of Dongguan and seized 450 tonnes of salt , the Shenzhen Special Zone Daily said on Tuesday . 
the newspaper said those involved had built a make-shift salt processing factory , made fake logos and bags and stored most of their salt in a warehouse less than 200 metres away from the Guangdong Provincial Salt Industry Corp office . 
it said the illegal salt would harm the city &apos;s salt market and affect the population &apos;s health , but gave no details .
