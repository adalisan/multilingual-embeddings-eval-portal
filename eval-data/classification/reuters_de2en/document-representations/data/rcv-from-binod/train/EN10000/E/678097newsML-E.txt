national statistics institute INSEE said on Friday industrial production , excluding construction , rose 3.0 percent in April from the previous month and was up 2.2 percent year-on-year . 
industrial production excluding energy , agri-food businesses and construction -- considered by INSEE the best measure of manufacturing output -- rose 1.1 percent in April from the previous month , giving a year-on-year rise of 3.7 percent . 
economists polled by Reuters on average forecast a 0.4 percent rise in industrial production in April .
