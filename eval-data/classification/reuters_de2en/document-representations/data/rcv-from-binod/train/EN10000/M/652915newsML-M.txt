Brazil &apos;s real dropped 0.06 percent in calm trading to close at 1.0731 per dollar on the commercial foreign exchange market on expectations of an upcoming decline in the real-dollar mini-band , dealers said . 
the current mini-band was set by the Central Bank on Monday at between 1.0720 and 1.0770 reais per dollar and players are expecting a 0.1 percent decline in coming days . 
in the interbank floating rate market , the real closed at 1.0776 per greenback , down 0.01 percent . 
in the parallel market , the real ended steady at 1.125 per dollar , traders said .
