Indian interest rates are expected to ease , Merrill Lynch said in a global economics and currencies report on Thursday . 
it said Indian interest rates have tended to be high due to strong private credit demand and government borrowing . 
&quot; The following developments could put downward pressure on interest rates , &quot; the Merrill report said . 
+ Further fiscal consolidation leading to lower government borrowing from the market . 
+ downward adjustment of inflationary expectations . 
+ Lower liquidity requirement enabling banks to have more funds available to meet credit demands , and 
+ Increased competition in the banking sector leading to downward pressure on interest margins .
