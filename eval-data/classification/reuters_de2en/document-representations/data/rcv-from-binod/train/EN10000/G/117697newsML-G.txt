five people were killed in a gun battle between communist outlaws in Bangladesh &apos;s western Jessore district , police said on Tuesday . 
they said the battle on Monday at Talbaria village involved activists of the Biplobi Communist Party ( BCP ) and the Sarbahara Party at Talbaria . 
the shootout lasted several hours , residents said . 
police say western Bangladesh is the base for half a dozen outlawed communist groups , who often fight among themselves . 
the Sarbahara ( Party of have-nots ) is the biggest group with hundreds of armed fighters , they said . 
the outlaws say they want to establish the rights of the poor through armed struggle , but police say their activities are confined mainly to robbery and murder .
