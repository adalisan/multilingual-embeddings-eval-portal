a South Korean riot police officer died of injuries after fierce clashes with students on Monday outside a Seoul university , state television reported . 
the officer had been beaten by students outside Hanyang University and was taken to hospital , where he died , the Korea Broadcasting System said . 
it identified the officer as Yoo Ji-woong . 
no other details were immediately available . 
witnesses said a number of police officers were beaten senseless by leftwing students armed with metal pipes and firebombs during protests to demand the resignation of President Kim Young-sam . 
injured officers were sprawled helplessly on the streets , their helmets scattered around them .
