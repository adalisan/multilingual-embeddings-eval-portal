Poland &apos;s gross domestic product ( GDP ) grew by 7.3 percent in the first quarter of 1997 against the same period of last year , the Central Statistical Office ( Gus ) said on Friday . 
Gus said in a statement the rise was mainly a result of strong growth in gross added value in the industrial and construction sectors . 
&quot; The growth was mainly a result of a high pace of growth in added gross value in the industry which reached 7.6 percent and a 19.5 percent growth in the construction sector against the weak first quarter of last year , &quot; Gus said .
