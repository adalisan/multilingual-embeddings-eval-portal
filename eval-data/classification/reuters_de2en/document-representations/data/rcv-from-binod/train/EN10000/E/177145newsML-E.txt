the Italian Treasury said it will offer new seven-year floating rate certificates ( CCTs ) bearing a first coupon of 3.80 percent at its regular mid-month auction . 
this compares with a first coupon of 4.55 percent at the previous auction of October 1 , 2003 CCTs . 
the new CCTs mature on November 1 , 2003 . 
the Treasury said it would issue further tranches of three , five and 10-year fixed rate bonds ( BTP ) . 
the Treasury said it would offer : 
* first tranche 1 / 11 / 96 7-yr CCTs paying an initial 3.80 PCT coupon . 
the Treasury the new coupon had a spread of 0.15 percent over the yield of six-month short-term bills ( Bots ) compared with a 0.3 percent spread previously . 
* seventh tranche 1 / 10 / 99 3-yr BTPs paying a 7.50 percent coupon . 
* seventh tranche 15 / 09 / 01 5-yr BTPs paying 7.75 percent coupon .
