the Bank of Israel has been intervening regularly in the market to defend the currency basket rate , which has been trading at the bottom end of its band for nearly two months .

the basket , composed of the dollar , mark , pound sterling , French franc and yen , was fixed at 3.6467 shekels against 3.6466 on Wednesday .
