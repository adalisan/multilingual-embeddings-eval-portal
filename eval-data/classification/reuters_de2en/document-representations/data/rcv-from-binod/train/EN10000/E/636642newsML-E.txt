Mexican Finance Minister Guillermo Ortiz reiterated on Wednesday he expected May 1997 to May 1998 inflation to come in around 13 percent . 
Ortiz , speaking in an interview with radio network Radio Red , said inflation in the next 12 months from June would be around 12-13 percent .
