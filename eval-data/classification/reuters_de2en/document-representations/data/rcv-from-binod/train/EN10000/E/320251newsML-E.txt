China has set the weighted average price for the first batch of 1997 state debt , a two-year discount note , at 82.39 yuan , the Shanghai Securities News reported on Monday . 
the weighted average underwriting price was established at a public tender held in Shanghai on Sunday , the newspaper said . 
Haitong Securities was chosen as the lead underwriter and Shenyin &amp; Wanguo Securities as the deputy lead underwriter for the issue . 
the note carries no coupon but was sold to the underwriters at a discount price of 82.39 yuan and will be redeemed at face value on maturity . 
the notes will be issued from January 22 to February 17 . 
China has yet to announce the total amount of state debt to be issued for 1997 , but industry analysts expect the sum to be a record .
