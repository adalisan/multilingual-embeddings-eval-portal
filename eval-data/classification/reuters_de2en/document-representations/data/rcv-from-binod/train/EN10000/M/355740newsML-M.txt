Philippine firms have invited tenders on Tuesday to buy 35,000 tonnes of U.S. wheat , traders said . 
the firms asked price quotes from commodity houses for the supply of 29,000 tonnes dark northern spring wheat and 6,000 tonnes of western white wheat . 
other details of the tenders were not immediately available .
