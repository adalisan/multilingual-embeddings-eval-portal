the European Union said on Wednesday it had donated 65 million ecus ( $ 80 million ) for Bosnia &apos;s emergency relief winter programmes in its biggest single contribution to the war-torn country . 
the European Community Humanitarian Office ( ECHO ) said in a statement issued in Sarajevo that it would be spending an additional 20 million ecus helping the needy in Croatia , Serbia and Montenegro . 
aiming to provide essential humanitarian aid and facilitate the return of refugees , ECHO channels its funds to the United Nations and 40 non-governmental agencies in Bosnia . 
the latest donations bring ECHO &apos;s funding to the region to more than 1.3 billion ecus , the statement said .
