Bangladeshi tea prices rose at the weekly auction on Tuesday on active buying by mostly overseas buyers , brokers said . 
they said buyers from Poland were most active while Pakistan and Jordan also lent a good support . 
bold and large brokens enjoyed increased demand from foreign buyers . 
some 963,490 kgs of teas were sold at the auction . 
the average price was 52.07 taka per kg , 1.78 taka up from previous sale . 
the next auction will be held on January 28 with an expected offer of 1.01 million kg . 
( $ 1 = 42.45 taka )
