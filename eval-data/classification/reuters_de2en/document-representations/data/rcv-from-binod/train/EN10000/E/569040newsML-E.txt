Poland &apos;s industrial climate in the manufacturing sector improved in April after a two-month fall as demand and output forecast became more opimistic , the Central Statistics Office ( Gus ) said on Thursday . 
in its monthly survey Gus said the industrial climate index increased to 10 points in April from four points in March and seven points in February . 
small and large companies were the most optimistic about future prospects , it said . 
Gus said the construction sector climate index increased to 52 points in April from 45 points in February , mainly as a result of better future orders . 
the retail sector climate index fell by four points to one points in April , pulled down by pessimism of state-owned companies . 
the indicator is a percentage balance between positive and negative responses from polled companies .
