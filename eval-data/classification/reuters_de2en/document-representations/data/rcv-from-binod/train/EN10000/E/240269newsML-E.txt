Belgium &apos;s central bank confirmed on Friday it revised its forecast for 1996 real Gross Domestic Product ( GDP ) growth to 1.4 percent from 1.2 percent . 
the Belgian Primary Dealers Association &apos;s newsletter said on Monday that the central bank had raised its 1996 forecast to 1.4 percent , from 1.2 percent in August . 
the 15 primary dealers on Monday also revised their 1996 GDP growth forecast to 1.5 percent from 1.3 percent in September . 
the European Commission in November forecast the Belgian economy would grow 1.4 percent in 1996 and 2.2 percent in 1997 .
