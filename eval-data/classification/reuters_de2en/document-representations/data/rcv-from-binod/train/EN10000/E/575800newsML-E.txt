Brazil &apos;s Indice Geral de Precos - Disponibilidade interna ( IGP-DI ) rose 0.59 percent in April , against a 1.16 percent advance in March , the independent research institute Getulio Vargas Foundation ( FGV ) said . 
the IGP-DI index is a compilation of three indices - the IPA wholesale price index , which rose 0.53 percent in the period ; the IPC consumer price index , which advanced 0.80 percent ; and the INCC national index of construction costs , which gained 0.23 percent .
