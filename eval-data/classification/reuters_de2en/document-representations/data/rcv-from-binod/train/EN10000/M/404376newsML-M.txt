the Israeli shekel representative rate was fixed almost unchanged against the dollar and the basket of key foreign currencies on Wednesday in lacklustre trading , dealers said . 
&quot; Trading was very quiet today and volume was relatively low . 
the shekel was traded in a tight range . 
the basket was also stable and the Bank of Israel was not seen in the market , &quot; said a dealer at Israel Discount Bank . 
the central bank has been intervening regularly in the market to defend the currency basket rate , which has been trading at the bottom end of its band for more than a month . 
&quot; The local market is waiting to see what the reaction will be to Federal Reserve Chairman Alan Greenspan &apos;s testimony before the U.S. Congress ( later ) on Wednesday , &quot; the dealer said . 
the representative rate for the dollar was fixed at 3.3380 shekels against 3.3390 on Tuesday . 
the basket , composed of the dollar , mark , pound sterling , French franc and yen , was fixed at 3.6418 shekels against 3.6411 on Tuesday . 
on the crosses the shekel traded against the yen at 2.7590 shekels per 100 yen against 2.7412 on Tuesday and against the mark at 1.9947 shekels against 1.9939 on Monday .
