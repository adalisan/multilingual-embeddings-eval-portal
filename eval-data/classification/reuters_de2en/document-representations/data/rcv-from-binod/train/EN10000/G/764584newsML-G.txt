Chatumongkol &apos;s transfer came less than one day after Bank of Thailand governor Rerngchai Marakanond tendered his resignation . 
Chatumongkol was the most senior civil servant at the ministry . 
Chatumongkol has previously called on the government to cut budgets and embrace additional fiscal and monetary discipline as the country adjusted to its most severe economic crisis in more than a decade . 
Chalerm told reporters after a special cabinet meeting in this northern province that the permanent secretary was transferred to an unspecified job at Government House and he would be temporarily replaced by his deputy , Supachai Pisitvanich .
