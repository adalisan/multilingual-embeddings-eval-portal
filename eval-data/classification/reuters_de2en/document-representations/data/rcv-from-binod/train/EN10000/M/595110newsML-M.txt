in the South , rice prices were steady , the U.S. Department of Agriculture said in its weekly rice cash market summary . 
long grain milled rice was selling mostly at $ 20.00 to $ 20.50 per cwt ; medium grain traded mostly at $ 18.50 to $ 19.00 ; second heads were steady and brewers rice prices were steady to $ 0.50 cents lower . 
rice by-products were higher , Bran was mostly steady to $ 5.00 higher , and millfeed was steady to $ 2.00 higher . 
in California , rice prices were steady with medium grain selling mostly from $ 18.00 to $ 19.00 , second heads were steady , Bran was unevenly $ 7.00 higher to $ 2.00 lower . 
California rice ship loadings : for the week ending May 17 , 1997 , at the Port of Sacramento there were no rice ship loadings . 
inspections of California rice for export : for the week ending May 17 , 1997 , there were no rice inspections . 
the yearly total since October 1 , 1996 is 9,476,475 cwt compared to 6,771,827 cwt a year ago .
