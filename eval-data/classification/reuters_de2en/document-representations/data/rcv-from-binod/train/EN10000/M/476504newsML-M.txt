1345 GMT - The Romanian leu currency ended Monday unchanged at about 6,700 / 7,000 to the dollar on a dormant interbank market , dealers said . 
* They said trading stayed thin amid low dollar demand , while money market short-term prices eased towards 200 percent from about 205 / 210 percent in the morning . 
* Most dealers said the leu should start a gentle slide in the coming weeks in order to make exports attractive . 
* Leu-dollar retail rates lingered at around 6,600 / 6,900 .
