Saudi Aramco has increased A-888 bunker diesel prices from Ras Tanura and Dammam by $ 3 a tonne to $ 238 a tonne with effect Janaury 9 , the company said . 
A-960 bunker fuel is left unchanged at $ 125 a tonne ( $ 19.19 a barrel ) and from Jeddah and Yanbu will be $ 129 a tonne ( $ 19.80 ) . 
A-888 from the Red Sea is $ 241 a tonne .
