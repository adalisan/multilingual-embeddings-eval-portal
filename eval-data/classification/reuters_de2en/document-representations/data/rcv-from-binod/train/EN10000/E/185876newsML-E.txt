Russian gross domestic product in the first 10 months of this year totalled 1,819 trillion roubles , which was six percent less than a year earlier in inflation-adjusted terms , the State Statistics Committee said on Wednesday . 
GDP in October was one percent lower than in September and six percent lower than a year earlier .
