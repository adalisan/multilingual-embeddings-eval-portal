the Italian Treasury is expected to issue between 29 and 31.5 trillion lire of short-term Bot bills at auction on July 27 , with an average forecast for 30.25 trillion lire against 33.25 trillion lire maturing , analysts polled by Reuters said on Tuesday . 
the Treasury is also seen issuing two trillion lire of both 18-month and 24-month zero coupon certificates ( CTZ ) , while there are no CTZs maturing at the end of the month . 
the Treasury will detail the amounts on offer at the end-month auction on Wednesday , while settlement date for the paper is July 31 .
