Graystone plc said on Friday that it has acquired Grove Products ( caravan accessories ) Ltd for an initial 1.25 million stg in cash and shares . 
the engineering and distribution group said the purchase price comprises 1.05 million stg in cash and 1,352,657 ordinary shares issued at a price of 14.79p. 
a further consideration of up to a maximum 350,000 stg will be payable based on the results for the year ending 31 December 1997 . 
grove is a distributor of caravan accessories .
