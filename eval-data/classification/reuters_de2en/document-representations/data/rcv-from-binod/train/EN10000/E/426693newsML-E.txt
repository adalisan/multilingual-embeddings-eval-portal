the volume of Hong Kong &apos;s domestic exports in December fell 10.0 percent on year , while re-exports rose 5.1 percent , census and Statistics Department data showed . 
total export volume rose 2.4 percent and import volume increased by 5.2 percent . 
volume of domestic exports for full-year 1996 was down 8.4 percent while volume of re-exports leapt 7.5 percent . 
total exports rose 4.8 percent and imports grew by 4.3 percent . 
prices of domestic exports rose 0.3 percent in December 1996 from a year earlier and re-export prices decreased 0.7 percent , import prices decreased by 1.7 percent . 
prices of domestic exports for full-year 1996 were 0.3 percent higher , while those of re-exports down 0.5 percent and import prices fell 1.3 percent . 
prices are based on unit values which do not take into account changes in the composition or quality of goods traded , except for some selected commodities for which specific price indices are available .
