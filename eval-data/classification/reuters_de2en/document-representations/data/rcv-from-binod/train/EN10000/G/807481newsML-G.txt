Saudi Arabia on Monday beheaded a Pakistani man for smuggling heroin into the kingdom , raising the number of people executed so far this year to 81 . 
an Interior Ministry statement published by the official Saudi Press Agency said the man was executed in the capital Riyadh after a court found him guilty of smuggling an unspecified quantity of heroin into the conservative kingdom . 
Saudi Arabia applies Islamic sharia law by publicly beheading by the sword convicted murderers , rapists , drug traffickers and other criminals .
