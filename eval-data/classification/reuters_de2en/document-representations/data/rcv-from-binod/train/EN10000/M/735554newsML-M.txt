the National Bank of Slovakia ( NBS ) on Thursday set a one-week repo tender to add market liquidity of a maximum 1.0 billion crowns . 
minimum rate in the tender was set at 8.8 percent , equal to the discount rate , a central bank statement said . 
average yield in the NBS one-week repo tender fell to 9.38 percent on Tuesday , from 17.25 percent in a similar repo on Monday . 
the central bank monitors market liquidity on a daily basis and decides each morning whether to hold any open market operations . 
according to NBS figures , the Slovak banking sector as of July 16 lacked some 5.97 percent of the funds needed to meet minimum reserves requirements on July 31 .
