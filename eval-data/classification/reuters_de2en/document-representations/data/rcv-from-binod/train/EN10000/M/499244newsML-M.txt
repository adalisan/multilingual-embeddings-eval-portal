the central Bank of Uganda ( BOU ) said on Thursday it sold eight billion shillings ( $ 7.7 million ) of 91-day treasury bills at its auction on Wednesday . 
BOU posted its annualised yield on the T-bills at 10.048 percent , an increase of 0.181 point over last week . 
it said the price range stood at 97.626 to 97 compared to 97.570 to 97.485 last week .
