the Chicago Stock Exchange ( CHX ) said on Wednesday that share volume in September rose to 296,517,522 , up 1.5 percent from 292,062,218 shares traded in September 1995 . 
Year-to-date share volume in 1996 stood at 2,884,999,504 , up 5.6 percent from the same nine-month period last year , when 2,733,029,223 shares changed hands , the CHX said .
