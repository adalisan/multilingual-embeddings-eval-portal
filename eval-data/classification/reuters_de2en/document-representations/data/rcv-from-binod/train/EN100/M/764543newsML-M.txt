state-owned Petroleum Authority of Thailand ( PTT ) has issued a tender to buy 80,000 tonnes of 180-cst fuel oil for September delivery , traders said on Tuesday . 
the tender , which seeks 6,000 tonnes of 1.0-percent sulphur and 74,000 tonnes of 2.0-percent fuel oil for delivery September 1-10 , closes on July 30 and is valid until July 31 . 
in its last purchase tender , PTT bought 75,000 tonnes of 2.0 percent sulphur and 5,000 tonnes of 1.0 percent fuel oil for August 15-25 delivery at $ 8.50 per tonne over Singapore spot quotes , cost-and-freight to Sri Racha .
