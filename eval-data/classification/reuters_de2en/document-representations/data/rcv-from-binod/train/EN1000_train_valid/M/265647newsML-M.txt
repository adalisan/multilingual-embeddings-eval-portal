Thai stocks closed at a 41-month-low on Wednesday as mainly foreign investors sold stocks on a belief that the market was unlikely to improve anytime soon , brokers said . 
investors sold because they did not believe that new Finance Minister Amnuay Viravan would be able to quickly turn around the tide for Thailand &apos;s slowing economy , they added . 
the SET Index fell 13.41 points , or 1.55 percent , to close at 853.82 points . 
the SET-50 closed at 64.44 down 1.29 points , or 1.96 percent in trade worth 3.784 billion baht . 
the index last closed below this level on June 11 , 1993 .
