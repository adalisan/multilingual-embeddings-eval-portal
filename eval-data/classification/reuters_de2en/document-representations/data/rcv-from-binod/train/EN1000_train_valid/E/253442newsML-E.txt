Irish inflation , as measured by the quarterly consumer price index , rose 1.9 percent in the year to mid-November compared with a 1.5 percent rise in the year to mid-August , the Central Statistics Office ( CSO ) said on Thursday .

the European Union interim index of consumer prices , which is used for intra-EU comparisons , showed a 0.5 percent rise over the quarter for an annual rise of 2.3 percent . 
the consumer price index excluding mortgage interest rose 0.4 percent over the quarter and 2.0 percent year-on-year .
