Sri Lanka &apos;s state-owned Ceylon Petroleum Corp ( CPC ) has issued a tender to buy diesel and jet fuel for December 12-13 delivery , on a cost-and-freight ( c-and-f ) basis to Colombo , a CPC source said on Wednesday . 
the tender seeks 120,000 barrels of 1.0-percent sulphur diesel and 80,000 barrels of aviation jet fuel . 
the tender closes at 1520 hours , Sri Lanka time on November 13 and offers are valid for two days . 
in its last tender which was awarded to Caltex Bahrain , CPC bought 160,000 barrels of 0.5-percent sulphur diesel at $ 1.77 per barrel over Singapore spot quotes , and 40,000 barrels of jet fuel at a $ 1.40-per-barrel premium . 
the combination cargo is for November 24-25 delivery to Colombo on a c-and-f basis and to Trincomalee at a later date .
