Swedish consumer prices are expected to be unchanged in August , giving an annual rate of 0.7 percent for the previous 12 months , a Reuters survey of 15 analysts showed on Thursday . 
analysts said a weaker Swedish crown would be reflected in the August figure , which is expected to be unchanged from a fall in July of 0.2 percent . 
the dip in July produced a year-on-year rise in consumer prices of 0.6 percent . 
in August 1995 consumer prices fell 0.1 percent compared to the previous month , for an annualised rate of 2.7 percent . 
the forecasts for August ranged from a fall of 0.2 percent to a rise of 0.2 percent , which give an annual forecast varying between 0.5 percent to 0.9 percent . 
statistics Sweden is due to publish the August figure on September 12 at 0630 GMT . 
analysts interviewed for the poll were from Carnegie , Consensus , Den Danske Bank , Deutsche Morgan Grenfell , Hagstromer &amp; Qviberg , Handelsbanken , JP Morgan , Morgan Stanley , Nomura , Nordbanken , Paine Webber , S-E-Banken , Swedbank , Transferator , and Union Bank of Switzerland .
