the Bank of Lebanon issued on Thursday certificates of deposit dated January 3 to commercial banks worth five billion Lebanese pounds . 
the Bank sold 45-day CDs worth one billion pounds at 12.25 percent annual interest and sold 60-day CDs worth four billion pounds carrying interest of 13 percent per annum . 
last week the Bank made no sales of 45-day or 60-day CDs as investors instead bought two-year treasury bills at 20.04 percent per annum , a central bank source has said .
