AUCKLAND , Feb 25 - Two newcomers from the Bay of plenty have been named in the Northern Districts team to play Sri Lanka in a two-day match starting on Thursday at the Owen Delaney Park , Taupo . 
Gavin Donne and Mark Tesar come into the side to be captained by wicketkeeper Robbie Hart . 
Northern District : Robert Hart ( captain ) , Mark Bailey , Matthew Bell , Dion Bennett , Grant Bradburn , Matthew Hart , Michael Parlane , Alex Tait , Joseph Yovich , Alex O &apos;Dowd , Gavin Donne , Mark Tesar .
