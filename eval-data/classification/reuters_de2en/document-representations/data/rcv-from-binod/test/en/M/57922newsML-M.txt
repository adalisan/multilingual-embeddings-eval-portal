the Johannesburg Stock Exchange was on Tuesday awash with positive sentiment , heaving South African industrial and gold shares to a strong finish . 
dealers said a batch of upbeat factors , including record gains on world markets and a steady rand , were responsible for the confident mood . 
continued futures-related basket trading , mainly on the March 1997 contract but also on the December 1996 contract , supported firmness . 
traders said overall market strength was seen continuing on Wednesday unless Wall Street came in for a pounding . 
the industrial index finished 26.2 points better at 8,211.7 elevating the overall index to 6,947.1 , 20.8 points up . 
the gold index ended 25.2 points firmer at 1,776.5 .
