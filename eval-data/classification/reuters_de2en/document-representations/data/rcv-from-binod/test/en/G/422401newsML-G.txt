South Korean president Kim Young-sam on Wednesday appointed a new finance minister , Kang Kyong-shik , a ruling New Korea Party lawmaker with extensive experience in government , Yonhap Television News reported . 
an official at the presidential Blue House confirmed the report . 
he said Kang would replace Han Seung-soo , who also served as deputy prime minister . 
the president also appointed Lim Chang-yuel to take over from Ahn Kwang-koo as trade minister . 
a total of seven ministerial portfolios changed hands in a reshuffle designed to help restore the president &apos;s political credibility badly damaged by a loans scandal involving bankrupt Hanbo Steel Co . 
other changes included the ministers for Home Affairs , Justice , Science and Technology , Culture and Sports , Construction and Transportation .
