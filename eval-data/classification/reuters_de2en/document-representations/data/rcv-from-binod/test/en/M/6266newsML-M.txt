Taiwan &apos;s Provincial Food Bureau will tender on August 28 for a 15,470-tonne shipment of U.S. No.1 wheat , grain traders said on Wednesday . 
the shipment would be slated for delivery from the Pacific Northwest between October 1 to 20 . 
traders said the Food Bureau was tendering on behalf of Taiwan &apos;s military and that such tenders usually are placed quietly once a year . 
traders said the shipment would comprise 7,700 tonnes of hard red winter wheat and 7,770 tonnes of another variety , apparently either soft wheat or spring wheat .
