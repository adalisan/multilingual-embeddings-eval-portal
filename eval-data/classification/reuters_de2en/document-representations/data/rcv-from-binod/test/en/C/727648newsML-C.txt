shares of Guangdong Investment Ltd rose on Monday on hopes of an exceptional gain after the company announced it would float its brewery business , brokers said .

the company said earlier on Monday it would float its Guangdong brewery Holdings Ltd unit by offering 350 million new shares , representing 28 percent of the enlarged share capital . 
about 216.62 million shares would be reserved for Guangdong Investment shareholders in the proportion of one brewery share for every eight parent shares held . 
trading in the brewery shares is due to begin in Hong Kong on August 8 .
