spot bulk electricity prices in the midwestern U.S. rose Monday as the region remained in the grip of frigid temperatures forecast to ease only slightly in the middle of the week , power brokers and marketers said . 
temperatures were forecast to rise somewhat to about five to 10 degrees below normal through portions of the Mid-West on Tuesday , but colder air was expected to again return on Thursday and last through the weekend , dropping temperatures back to 10 to 15 degrees below normal . 
in the Mid-Continent Area Power pool ( MAPP ) , peak , non-firm power prices for Tuesday delivery were discussed at $ 34.00-37.00 from $ 28.00-32.00 per megawatt hour ( MWh ) late last week , the brokers and marketers said . 
off-peak next day non-firm MAPP power also moved higher to $ 15.00-17.00 from $ 10.50-12.00 late last week . 
in the Mid-American Interpool Network ( main ) , peak , non-firm power prices for Tuesday were talked in the $ 35.00-39.00 range from $ 28.00-30.50 late last week , the sources said . 
main off peak next day power jumped to $ 16.50-18.50 from $ 13.00-15.00 last week , the sources said . 
peak , non-firm electricity prices in the East Central Area Reliability Coordination Agreement ( ECAR ) for Tuesday were discussed higher at $ 35.00-38.00 from $ 28.00-30.00 last week . 
ECAR electricity prices for off-peak , non-firm delivery were notionally assessed higher at $ 15.00-19.00 from $ 13.00-15.00 per MWh , the sources said . 
February ECAR was assessed steady to higher at $ 23.75-24.25 from $ 23.50-24.00 last week . 
in the Southwest Power pool ( spp ) peak , non-firm power prices for Tuesday delivery were talked at $ 34.00-36.00 from $ 29.00-33.00 late last week , the sources said . 
off-peak prices throughout spp for Tuesday delivery rose to $ 20.00-22.50 from $ 18.00-20.00 in Friday discussion , the sources said . 
February spp was said to have held steady at $ 24.00-25.00 , the sources said . 
separately the 1,205 megawatt ( MW ) Perry nuclear power plant in North Perry , Ohio , was expected to reach full power Monday after tripping off line Tuesday due to an electrical problem . 
also , IES Utilities said Monday it expects its 538 MW Duane Arnold nuclear power plant in Palo , Iowa to resume electricity production Thursday following a repair shutdown over the weekend .
