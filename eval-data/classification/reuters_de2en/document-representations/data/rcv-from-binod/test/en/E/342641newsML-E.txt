China has uncovered more than 384.3 billion yuan in non-declared income received by local governments in 1995 , the Shanghai Securities News said on Wednesday . 
the income was discovered in investigations conducted by the disciplinary department of the Communist Party last year , the newspaper said . 
the investigations found some local governments to be abusing their powers by illegally establishing funds , levying unauthorised taxes and approving unnecessary projects to raise money , the newspaper said . 
&quot; Quite a number of local governments , ranging from the provincial to the village level , were found to be involved in such acts to some extent , &quot; the newspaper said . 
the disciplinary department had punished 470 officials for irregularities and another 149 officials had been brought to court as a result of the investigation , the newspaper said . 
( $ 1 = 8.3 yuan )
