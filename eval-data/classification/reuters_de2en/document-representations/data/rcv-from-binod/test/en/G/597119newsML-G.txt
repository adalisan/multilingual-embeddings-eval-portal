South African Minister of Safety and Security Sydney Mufamadi told parliament on Tuesday that 506 banks were robbed in the 10 months to the end of 1996 . 
the highest number of robberies was reported in Gauteng , around Johannesburg , where 193 banks were robbed , he said in a written response to a question . 
ninety three banks were robbed in KwaZulu-Natal , 48 in Northwest province , 45 in the Eastern Cape and 28 in the Western Cape .
