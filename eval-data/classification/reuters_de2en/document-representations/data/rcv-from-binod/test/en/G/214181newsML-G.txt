an undated silver Hong Kong dollar coin bearing the bust of British Queen Victoria was sold for HK $ 828,000 ( US $ 108,000 ) at an auction here , London-based auctioneer Spink &amp; sons Ltd said on Monday . 
the second highest price of the auction went for a 1929 silver dollar coin showing the bust of Sun Yet Sen , founder of modern China , at HK $ 483,000 , it said . 
the top-selling banknote was a Hongkong and Shanghai Bank $ 5 note , dated May 1 , 1884 , which sold for HK $ 356,500 , the auctioneer said . 
Hong Kong , a British colony for more than 150 years , reverts to Chinese rule in 1997 .
