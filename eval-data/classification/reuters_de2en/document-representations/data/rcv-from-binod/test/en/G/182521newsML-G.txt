Iran has executed six men for belonging to a gang that carried out several armed robberies , killing one man and injuring several policemen , a newspaper said on Tuesday . 
the daily Kayhan quoted a Tehran Revolutionay Court as saying the six men were executed in the Evin prison outside the capital after the Supreme Court upheld the death sentences . 
it did not say when the executions were carried out . 
Mohammad Reza Golpaygani , identified as the gang &apos;s leader , was convicted of killing one man and robbing passengers of two buses , the newspaper said . 
one man was convicted of taking part in five armed robberies and shooting and injuring an unspecified number of policemen during his arrest , it added . 
the four other gang members were convicted of several armed robberies at homes , restaurants , a transportation firm and a savings and loans office .
