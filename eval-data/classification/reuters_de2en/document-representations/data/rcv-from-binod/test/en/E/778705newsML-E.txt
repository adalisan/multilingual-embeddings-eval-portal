Chilean consumer prices rose 0.6 percent last month , bringing the rise in inflation in the first seven months of the year to 3.1 percent , said the National Statistics Institute ( INE ) . 
the INE figures showed inflation rising 5.6 percent over the 12 months to July . 
consumer prices rose 0.3 percent in July last year , with an increase of 7.7 percent in the year ending July 1996 . 
prices rose 4.1 percent in the first seven months of last year . 
inflation was stoked last month by a sharp 1.6 percent increase in food prices , a 0.8 percent rise in the &quot; other &quot; category and a 0.1 percent rise in transport and communications costs . 
analysts had been expecting vegetable prices to rise last month as a result of flooding in June which destroyed crops in agricultural areas near Santiago . 
the figures showed clothing prices fell 0.9 percent in July , while housing costs dropped 0.3 percent .
