Pakistani stocks finished at level on Friday on profit-taking by jobbers in a directionless market before the Saturday-Sunday weekend , dealers at the Karachi Stock Exchange ( KSE ) said . 
the KSE 100-share index closed 0.97 point , or 0.06 percent , lower at 1,551.83 amid 159 losers and 73 gainers in a provisional turnover of 36 million . 
Jobbers unloaded speculative stocks and booked profits in the directionless market before the weekend , one dealer said .
