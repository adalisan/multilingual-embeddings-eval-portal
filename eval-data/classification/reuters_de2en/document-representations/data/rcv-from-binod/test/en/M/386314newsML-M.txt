the average accepted yield of one-year Exchange Fund bills was 5.14 percent at a tender on Tuesday , the Hong Kong Monetary Authority said . 
this compares with 12-month HIBOR of 6.13942 percent at 0300 GMT and the average accepted yield of 5.13 percent at the previous tender on Jan 21 . 
the average accepted yield of the 91-day bills was 4.78 percent compared to 4.53 percent at the last tender , and three-month HIBOR of 5.64904 percent . 
the average accepted yield for the 28-day bills was 4.83 percent compared to one-month HIBOR at 5.53846 percent and 4.47 percent at the last tender .
