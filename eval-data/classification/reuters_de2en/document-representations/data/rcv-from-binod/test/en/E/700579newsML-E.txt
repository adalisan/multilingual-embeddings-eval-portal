the Thai consumer price index ( CPI ) rose 4.4 percent year-on-year in June against a 4.3 percent rise in May , the Commerce Ministry said on Tuesday . 
the ministry said the 1990-based index rose to 139.1 in June from its 133.2 level in the same month a year ago . 
the Thai Commerce Ministry issued the following economic indicators :

year / year + 4.4 + 4.3 + 5.5

year / year + 5.7 + 5.3 + 8.5 
non food / beverage Index 131.7 131.5 127.4 
month / month + 0.2 + 0.7 + 0.3 
year / year + 3.4 + 3.5 + 3.5 
notes : in the food and beverage category , prices of non-alcoholic drinks surged 4.0 percent in June from May , rice and flour products gained 2.1 percent , meat , poultry and dairy products rose 0.6 percent , while vegetables and fruit prices contracted 4.2 percent . 
in the non-food group , entertainment , reading materials and educational costs rose 0.9 percent , medical and personal care costs increased 0.3 percent , while transport and communications expenses fell 0.2 percent .
