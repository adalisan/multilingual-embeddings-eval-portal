the Moroccan Bank al-Maghrib ( central bank ) injected 500 million dirhams in the money market for one week at its repurchase auction on Wednesday , a bank official said . 
the bank said it received bids totalling 7.108 billion dirhams and allocated 2.500 billion dirhams at seven percent interest rate for one week . 
the amount allocated was less than 2.000 billion dirhams leaving the market this week , it said . 
the bank satisfied 35.17 percent of demand . 
the next auction is due to be held on September 11 , 1996 . 
( $ 1 = 8.6 dirhams )
