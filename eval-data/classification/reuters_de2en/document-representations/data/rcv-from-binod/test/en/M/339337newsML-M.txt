Taiwan &apos;s central bank on Tuesday injected T $ 6.303 billion worth of funds into the banking system through open market operations , a central bank official said . 
the bank , through three bills finance companies , bought T $ 6.303 billion in approved securities instruments offered by financial institutions with yield at between 5.375 and 5.450 percent , the official said . 
the institutions have the option of repurchasing the instruments between three and 29 days from now , the official said .
