Danish industrial sales rose four percent in current prices during October-December 1996 and orders rose three percent , compared to the same period last year , the national statistics bureau said on Friday . 
the manufacturing price index rose by one percent in October-December 1996 compared to the same period last year , while the quantity index , indicating trends in turnover of goods and services , showed a two percent year-on-year gain . 
the data was not seasonally adjusted .
