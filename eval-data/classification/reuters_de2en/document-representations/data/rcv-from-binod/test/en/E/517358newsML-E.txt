Hong Kong &apos;s index of industrial production decreased by 3.5 percent in 1996 , the census and Statistics department said on Thursday . 
textiles output was down 6.3 percent and that of electrical and electronic products , machinery , professional equipment and optical goods fell 5.1 percent . 
consumer electrical and electronic products output decreased by 11.0 percent . 
chemicals , rubber , plastic and non-metallic mineral products output rose 0.1 percent . 
food , beverages and tobacco output decreased 1.0 percent while paper products and printing output fell 1.9 percent . 
basic metals and fabricated metal products output decreased 8.9 percent and that of machinery , equipment , apparatus , parts and components rose 0.1 percent . 
production of wearing apparel , except footwear , fell 4.3 percent . 
the index of industrial production for the fourth quarter of 1996 decreased by 3.0 percent over the same quarter of 1995 .
