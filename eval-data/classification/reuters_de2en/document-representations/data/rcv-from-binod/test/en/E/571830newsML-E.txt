Brazilian federal tax receipts were 9.668 billion reais in April , up 21.62 percent from the 7.949 billion reais collected in the same month last year , Internal Revenue head Everardo Maciel said . 
Maciel told reporters the April result was a 2.28 percent gain in real terms over the 9.328 billion reais collected in March . 
Brazil &apos;s National Treasury was expected to release April &apos;s expenses and the Treasury &apos;s financial results for the month sometime over the next two weeks .
