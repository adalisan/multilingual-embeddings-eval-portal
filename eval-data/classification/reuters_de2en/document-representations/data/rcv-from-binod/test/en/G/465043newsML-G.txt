shots were fired at an Italian coastguard boat from a trawler carrying Albanian refugees in the southern Adriatic on Monday , the Italian news agency Ansa reported . 
the news agency quoted the commander of the coastguard in the southern port of Brindisi , Capt Giovanni Biso , as saying there were no injuries in the incident . 
another coastguard official said he could not immediately confirm the report . 
the coastguard official said they were seeking more information on the incident from the captain of the patrol boat . 
the agency said it occured outside the port of Brindisi . 
Brindisi is where many of the nearly 12,000 Albanian refugees have arrived on a flotilla of rusty , old boats since March 13 to escape the chaos in their country .
