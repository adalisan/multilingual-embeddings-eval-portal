private sector average weekly ordinary time earnings ( AWOTE ) fell 0.1 percent in the three months to May for a rise of 2.5 percent on the year , the Australian Bureau of Statistics said . 
public sector AWOTE rose 1.6 percent in the quarter to be up 7.2 percent over the previous 12 months . 
final AWOTE data for adults employed full time was revised to a rise of 0.3 percent from 0.5 percent , giving an annual rise of 3.5 percent .
