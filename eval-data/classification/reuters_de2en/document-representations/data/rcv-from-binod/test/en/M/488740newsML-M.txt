Malaysian blue-chip stocks extended their losses on Friday afternoon with the key blue-chip index down 3.03 percent on continued selling of banking and property shares , brokers said . 
at 0648 GMT the benchmark composite Index of 100 large-capitalised issues was down 34.91 points to 1,117.60 
retail investors were hit by cash calls as their holdings fell in value and were asked to top-up their borrowings by their brokers , dealers and analysts said .
