eight people were killed and seven seriously injured in a northern Philippine town when a hand grenade exploded , police said on Monday . 
they said a group of men were drinking on a streetcorner in Mapandan , Pangasinan province , on Saturday evening when an unidentified person lobbed what the men first thought was a piece of rock . 
after recovering the object , they took it into the light in front of a store to see what it was when it exploded , killing two of the men and six bystanders -- two women , three girls and a female child . 
police said they had arrested the suspected thrower of the grenade and were questioning him .
