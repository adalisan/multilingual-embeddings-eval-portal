soybean prices were steady in central Indian markets in very limited trading activity on Thursday , dealers said . 
soybean arrivals were poor due to the close of the financial year as purchasers had run out of funds . 
soybean markets will be closed for Good Friday and will open on Monday . 
Soyoil solvent was down by 100 rupees per tonne on selling pressure from plants . 
demand from hydrogenated vegetable oil manufacturing units was limited . 
business volume was dull and the undertone was steady . 
soymeal prices were strong in local market on better demand but prices were steady in export . 
soymeal yellow was $ 304-305 per tonne FAS ( Free alongside Ship ) in bulk for export . 
rapeseed extraction was $ 115-116 per tonne FAS ( Free alongside Ship ) in bulk for export . 
rapeseed extraction was 4,000 rupees per tonne in for ( Free on Rail ) condition .
