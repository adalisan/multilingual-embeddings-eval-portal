Morocco &apos;s Bank al-Maghrib ( central bank ) withdrew 468 million dirhams from the money market for one week at its repurchase rate agreeement auction , a bank official said on Thursday . 
the bank received bids totalling 3.880 billion dirhams but allocated only 2.700 billion dirhams at 6.5 percent interest rate . 
that was less than the 3.168 billion dirhams leaving the market , the bank said . 
it satisfied 69.59 percent of the demand for liquidity . 
the next auction is due in November 27 , 1996 .
