the Pakistani rupee opened 0.02 down in the kerb market at 39.30 to the dollar on Wednesday on rumours , officially denied , of a possible devaluation , dealers said . 
one dealer said the rupee was likely to close lower on higher demand for the dollar from investors . 
the State ( central ) Bank of Pakistan rate , last changed on August 29 , was 35.6200 / 35.7981 . 
an official at the bank denied that any rupee devaluation was planned . 
the premium on foreign exchange bearer certificates opened unchanged at 10.00 percent .
