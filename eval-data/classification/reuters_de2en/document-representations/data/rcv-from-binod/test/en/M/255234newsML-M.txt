CBOT soymeal futures closed higher on talk China bought soymeal from South America and the U.S. , traders said . 
there was news in late dealings that China bought 150,000 tonnes of soymeal from South America and there was talk China bought 50,000 from the U.S. , they said . 
Soyoil closed higher on late short covering . 
Soyoil also was underpinned by talk that the average soyoil content for the 1996 U.S. soybean crop was declining to around 10.8 to 10.9 percent from the usual 11 percent or above level . 
soymeal futures volume was estimated at 18,000 contracts , below the 28,250 traded Thursday , according to the CBOT . 
Soyoil futures volume was estimated at 15,000 contracts , below the 19,082 traded Thursday .
