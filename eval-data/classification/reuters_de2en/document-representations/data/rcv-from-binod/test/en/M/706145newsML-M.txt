Danish share turnover rose by 54 percent in the first six months of 1997 compared to first-half 1996 , with bond turnover only one percent up on the same period last year , the Danish Securities Centre reported on Thursday . 
the centre said that share turnover for the period totalled 75 billion crowns in nominal value , the equivalent of 620 million crowns per trading day . 
it said bond turnover for the half-year amounted to a nominal 10,558 billion crowns , corresponding to 87 billion crowns per trading day with 70 percent of bond trading and 16 percent of share trading taking place between securities dealers .
