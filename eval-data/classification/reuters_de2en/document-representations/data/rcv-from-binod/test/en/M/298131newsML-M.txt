shares on the unofficial United Arab Emirates stock market ended the week to Thursday mostly steady in slow trade in the first week of the new year , dealers said . 
the National Bank of Abu Dhabi ( NBAD ) 27-share index rose just over five points to 2,492.34 from 2,486.93 . 
the dealers said quiet trading was limited to few shares of banks and some companies . 
the Commercial Bank of Dubai shares climbed two dirhams to 60 dirhams . 
Emirates Insurance Company shares gained 10 dirhams to 630 dirhams . 
Abu Dhabi National Hotels Co lost 10 dirhams to 1,035 . 
most other traded shares were flat .
