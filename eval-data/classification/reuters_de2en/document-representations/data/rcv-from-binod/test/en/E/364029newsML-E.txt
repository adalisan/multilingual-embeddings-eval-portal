Iraq has agreed to pay back a $ 53 million debt to the Greek state and the Greek powder and cartridge Company ( Pyrkal ) , Defence Undersecretary Dimitris Apostolakis told parliament on Thursday . 
Parliament sources confirmed that Apostolakis answered an opposition member &apos;s question on the issue and reassured Greek parliament members that an agreement between Greece and Iraq had been reached . 
the payment will start with installments in kind , namely Iraq will provide Greece with crude oil and its derivative products in a first effort to repay its debt , the Athens News Agency ( Ana ) reported . 
the remaining amount will be paid in cash after the embargo against Baghdad is lifted Ana said .
