Bangladeshi tea prices remained mostly steady on the active presence of local and traditional overseas buyers at the weekly auction in Chittagong on Tuesday , brokers said . 
they said Pakistan , Afghanistan , Poland and Russia were in the market which saw active support from local buyers , especially from blenders of good liquoring smaller brokens tea . 
some 0.8 million kg of teas were sold at the auction . 
the average price was 49.96 taka per kg , 0.05 taka down from previous level . 
the next auction will be held on January 14 with an expected offer of 935,000 kg .
