late North Sea Brent paper markets gained in value Friday , lifted higher when two cargoes were bought by a Wall Street house and a refiner . 
traders said a full paper cargo was sold at $ 18.15 and a second parcel at $ 18.16 , a shade firmer than the IPE settlement price for August Brent at $ 18.12 a barrel . 
the IPE market declined some 57 cents on the end to supply concerns when Hurricane Danny passed through the U.S. Gulf without incident , and on the resumption of Colombian Cano Limon production . 
smaller partial trades were noted for August Brent at $ 18.18 and $ 18.17 a barrel , traders said .
