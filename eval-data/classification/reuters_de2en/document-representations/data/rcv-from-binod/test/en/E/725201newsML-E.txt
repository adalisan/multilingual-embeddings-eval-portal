the Latvian central bank said on Monday that its foreign assets fell in June by 1.2 million lats to 532.602 million lats . 
the bank said in a statement that its foreign liabilities also fell , by 3.3 million lats to 134.956 million lats . 
it said that both falls came as the bank continued to pay back International Monetary Fund ( IMF ) credits . 
the amount of lats in circulation came to 310.04 million lats , a rise of 13.7 million lats or 4.6 percent due to a seasonal rise in demand .
