Louisiana &apos;s East baton Rouge Parish voted to extend a half-cent sales tax for road work for five years , the Advocate reported . 
the extension was approved by nearly 70 percent of those voting in a Saturday referendum . 
the tax extension is expected to raise $ 130 million over five years , with $ 78 million earmarked for 10 road construction projects and the remainder allocated for repairs .
