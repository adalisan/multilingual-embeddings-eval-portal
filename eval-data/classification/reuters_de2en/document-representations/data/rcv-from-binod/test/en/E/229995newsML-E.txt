inflation in Malaysia , as measured by the consumer price index ( CPI ) , is forecast at 3.53 percent in 1997 the same as this year , a Reuter poll of major research houses said . 
the poll of 16 research houses forecast an average 3.53 percent rise in the CPI in 1996 and 1997 against a 3.4 percent rise announced by the government for last year . 
the government has forecast an inflation rate of 3.6 percent in 1996 . 
nine of the research houses polled provided forecasts for the fourth quarter of 1996 and first quarter of 1997 . 
they forecast an average 3.54 percent rise in the CPI in the fourth quarter of this year and 3.59 percent in the first quarter of next year .
