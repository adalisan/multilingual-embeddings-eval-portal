oriental Land Co , the operator of Tokyo Disneyland , plans to list its shares on the second section of the Tokyo Stock Exchange in early December . 
it hopes to issue around 15 million to 18 million new shares and sell around six to eight million existing shares . 
oriental may see a capital infusion surpassing the record 172.3 billion yen raised by Japan Telecom Co Ltd when it listed on the second section in September 1994 . 
Shin-Etsu Chemical Co Ltd plans to build a vinyl chloride resin plant in the United States . 
it hopes to start plant construction later this month through its U.S. subsidiary Shintech Inc . 
it is expected to invest about $ 700 million and hopes to start operations in mid-1998 . 
machine tool manufacturers are taking steps to shrink the gap between suggested and actual selling prices by introducing an open price system . 
medium-sized machine tool maker Enshu Ltd plans to cut prices by about 20 percent on some models except new products from late October . 
citizen Watch Co Ltd also plans to increase the number of machine tools on which it offers no discount . 
star Micronics Co Ltd , a leading electronic printer maker , plans to transfer production of about 30 percent of its machine tools , such as some automatic lathes , to its subsidiary in China . 
it hopes to start production from autumn 1997 and to export about 300 units annually to Japan . 
Mitsubishi Oil Co Ltd hopes to cut about 100 management staff by December under its early retirement programme , due to a sharp decline in the oil and gas prices . 
Oji Paper Co plans to invest 3.2 billion yen to boost production of adhesive papers as part of its plan to gain a larger share of the growing market . 
it hopes to start operations in 1997 . 
Daiei Inc plans to launch Japan &apos;s largest supermarket in Osaka , with a size of about 30,000 sq meters . 
it hopes to start operations in April 1999 . 
Nippon Steel Semiconductor Corp a semiconductor maker and group company of Nippon Steel Corp , plans to boost production of 64-megabit dynamic random-access memory chips in late 1998 . 
the Nippon Steel unit plans initial investment of 10 billion to 15 billion yen .
