Pakistani cotton rates closed steady on Wednesday due to a lack of demand from well-stocked spinners , dealers said . 
in the kerb market , the rate for NIAB-78 variety was quoted at 2,225 to 2,275 rupees per maund ( 37.32 kg ) , a dealer at the private Karachi Cotton Association ( KCA ) said . 
he said the rate was likely to open stable on Thursday .
