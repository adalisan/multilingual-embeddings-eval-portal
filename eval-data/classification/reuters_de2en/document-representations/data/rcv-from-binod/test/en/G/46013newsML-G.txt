Albania will host a European festival of mediaeval music next year in the southern town of Gjirokaster , an official said on Wednesday . 
Roland Cene , head of the cultural centre in Gjirokaster , 230 km ( 145 miles ) south of Tirana , said participants would come from Germany , France , Italy , Croatia , Austria , Spain and Hungary , as well as Albania itself .
