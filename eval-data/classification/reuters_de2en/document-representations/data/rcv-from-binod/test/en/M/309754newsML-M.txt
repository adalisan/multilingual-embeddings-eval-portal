from the Gulf , A-960 bunker fuel oil is dropped $ 5 a tonne to $ 120 ( $ 18.42 a barrel ) . 
from Jeddah and Yanbu A-960 bunker fuel oil is also $ 5 lower at $ 124 a tonne ( $ 19.03 a barrel ) .
