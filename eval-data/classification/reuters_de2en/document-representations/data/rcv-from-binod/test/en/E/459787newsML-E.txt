the Italian city of Naples said its March consumer price index stood at 0.1 percent month on month and 2.8 percent year on year . 
in February , Naples &apos; inflation rate was 0.2 percent and 3.0 percent respectively . 
the general index and the housing and utility charges sector have been revised to take account of a court ruling that reversed electricity tariff reductions . 
Naples is one of 11 Italian cities to have published inflation data Thursday and Friday . 
the 11 sample cities have a combined weighting of 75.66 percent on the total index .
