Argentine consumer prices rose 0.2 percent in July compared with 0.5 percent in July last year and a 0.2 percent rise in June this year , the Economy Ministry said Monday . 
wholesale prices dropped 0.4 percent in July , compared with an 0.1 percent decline in July 1996 and a 0.4 percent decline a month earlier . 
annualized consumer inflation in July was 0.6 percent , compared with zero inflation in July last year . 
there was accumulated deflation of 0.1 percent in the first seven months of the year . 
for wholesale prices , July showed annualized deflation of 0.4 percent , while there were accumulated wholesale price declines of 0.8 percent in the first seven months of the year . 
analysts polled by Reuters had predicted consumer prices would rise 0.2 percent in the month .
