Chesterfield properties plc said on Wednesday that it had acquired a long lease of the Ramada Hotel complex in Manchester from P &amp; O Property Holdings for 9.6 million stg . 
the complex includes the 205 bedroom Ramada Hotel , 2,600 square metres of offices and 1,033 square metres of retail and leisure uses . 
rent for the whole complex is currently 1.05 million stg , showing an initial yield of 10.64 percent . 
&quot; Opportunity exists for rent increase and the bringing into use of existing and new space with greater income potential , &quot; Chesterfield said . 
Manchester City Council is the freeholder .
