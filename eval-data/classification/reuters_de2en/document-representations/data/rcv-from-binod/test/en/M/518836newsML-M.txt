Austrian traders have submitted bids for 9,000 tonnes of free market wheat and 1,050 tonnes of free market barley at Thursday &apos;s EU grains tender , member state officials said . 
the bids on wheat are for a refund ranging from 0.00 to 0.50 Ecus per tonne , the officials said . 
on barley a refund of 27.10 Ecus is sought . 
the EU cereals management committee will adjudicate tender bids at its meeting on Thursday afternoon .
