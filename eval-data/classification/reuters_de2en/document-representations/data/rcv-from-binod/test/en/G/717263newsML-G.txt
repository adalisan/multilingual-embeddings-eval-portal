Qatar said a Pakistani and a Sri Lankan convicted of murder and other offences under an Islamic sharia law were executed by firing squad on Wednesday . 
an Interior Ministry statement said Hafez Abdulghafour Fayez , a Pakistani , was convicted of murdering a Pakistani woman , and of theft and adultery . 
Sri Lankan Sunil Santha Kadigamuwa was convicted of murdering a Qatari national , attempted murder and rape . 
an Interior Ministry official who asked not to named said capital punishment was not common in Qatar . 
&quot; I cannot remember when was the last time we had an execution here , &quot; he told Reuters .
