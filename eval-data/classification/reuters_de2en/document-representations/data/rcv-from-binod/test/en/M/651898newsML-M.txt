Vietnam will auction 70 billion Dong ( $ 5.9 million ) worth of one-year Treasury notes on Wednesday , an official at the State Bank of Vietnam said on Tuesday . 
at the last sale of government paper , on June 4 , the central bank sold 70 billion Dong worth of one-year Treasury notes to domestic institutions at an annual interest rate of 11.90 percent . 
the government has held 11 Treasury paper auctions so far this year , through which a total of 449.4 billion Dong worth of Treasury stock has been issued .
