the benchmark 48-hour peso and its 24-hour counterpart opened 1.25 and 1.2 centavos firmer , respectively , at 7.9670 / 7.9700 and 7.9650 / 7.9680 per dollar . 
same-day peso contracts had meanwhile begun the session 1.6 centavos firmer at 7.9560 / 7.9630 per dollar . 
Banco de Mxico announced after the close of trade on Monday the consumer price index had risen by 0.91 percent in May , the first time it had done so by less than one percent in a month since the devaluation crisis of December 1994 . 
the central bank will hold its weekly auction of government securities on Tuesday evening .

dealers said a stronger U.S. 30-year Treasury bond , up 5 / 32 in early trade to yield 6.81 percent , also bolstered the peso .
