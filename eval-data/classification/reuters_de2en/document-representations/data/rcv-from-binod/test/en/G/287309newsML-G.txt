Australia captain Mark Taylor won the toss and elected to bat against West Indies in a World Series limited overs match at the Gabba on Sunday . 
Australia were without fast Bowler Glenn McGrath because of swelling on his left knee after he was hit by a delivery during batting practice on Saturday . 
McGrath was replaced by fellow fast Bowler Anthony Stuart , who is making his international debut . 
Shivnarine Chanderpaul returned to West Indies &apos; middle order after missing his side &apos;s last match because of an infection in his mouth . 
Australia , West Indies and Pakistan are locked together on four points from as many games in the triangular competition .
