an earthquake jolted parts of the Caribbean sister islands of Trinidad and Tobago on Wednesday , authorities said . 
there were no reports of injuries or fatalities from the quake , which struck at around 2 : 15 a.m. local time ( 0615 GMT ) , but there was structural damage to buildings on both Trinidad and Tobago . 
Tobago was the hardest-hit , with damage to government buildings and supermarkets in the capital of Scarborough . 
the epicentre of the quake , which registered 5.6 on the open-ended Richter scale , was 25 miles ( 40 km ) west of Scarborough , according to the seismic Unit of the University of the West Indies .
