Mexican overnight Cetes rates closed up 50 basis points at 18.5 percent on Friday on what dealers said was caution after the central bank increased the supply of paper for the primary auction . 
Banco de Mexico increased the amount of Cetes on auction for the second straight week , and will offer 4.2 billion pesos worth next Tuesday at its primary auction . 
since February , the weekly amount has been 3.0 billion pesos until this month . 
Bank paper closed at 19.0 percent , a hike of 50 basis points compared to the previous close .
