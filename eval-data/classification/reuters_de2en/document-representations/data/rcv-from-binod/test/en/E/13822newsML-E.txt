Canadian Mortgage and Housing Corporation , a federally guaranteed issuing agency , issued on Monday a C $ 65 million 6.25 percent bond due September 4 , 2001 at a retail price of C $ 99.249 , lead manager RBC dominion Securities Inc said . 
RBC Securities said the issue was set with a yield to investors of 6.426 percent on a monthly basis and 6.513 percent on a semi-annual basis . 
the issue was priced to yield less than the Government of Canada 7.0 percent bond due 2001 , said Coleen Miller , deputy treasury with CMHC .
