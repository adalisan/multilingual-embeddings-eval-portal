the Tokyo commodity Exchange ( TOCOM ) said on Wednesday that it would raise the margin requirement on its September rubber futures contract by 10,000 yen per trading lot , effective from Thursday . 
this will bring the total margin requirement on the September contract to 157,750 yen . 
the move aims to cool overheated trade . 
margin fees on the October contract will remain unchanged at 62,500 yen , and the fees on the other contracts will also be unchanged , at 52,500 yen , TOCOM said .
