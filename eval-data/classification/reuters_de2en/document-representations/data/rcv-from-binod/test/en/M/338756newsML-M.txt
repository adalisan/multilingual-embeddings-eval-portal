yields varied on one-month to one-year treasury bills worth two billion riyals offered by the Saudi Arabian Monetary Agency ( SAMA ) on Tuesday , bankers said . 
the yield on 28-day bills decreased to 5.3585 percent from 5.3965 last week and the rate on 91-day paper rose to 5.4836 from 5.4677 percent . 
the yield on 182-day bills fell to 5.6097 from 5.6697 percent . 
the rate on 364-day bills increased to 5.8774 from 5.8687 percent last week . 
the bills were priced at 99.58496 , 98.63281 , 97.24219 and 94.39063 respectively . 
one-month interbank deposit rates were put at 5-1 / 2 , 3 / 8 percent , three months were at 5-5 / 8 , 1 / 2 percent and six months were at 5-3 / 4 , 5 / 8 percent . 
one-year interbank rates were quoted at six , 5-7 / 8 percent , or 1 / 16 point higher with some banks .
