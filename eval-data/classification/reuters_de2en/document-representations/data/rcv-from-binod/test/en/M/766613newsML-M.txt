Reuters said Wednesday it calculated its 30-day visible supply of upcoming municipal bond sales at $ 6.843 billion , up $ 484 million from Tuesday . 
negotiated sales total $ 4.955 billion in 42 issues and competitive bond sales total $ 1.889 billion in 121 issues . 
Reuters visible supply counts all deals underwriters have either scheduled for sale in the next 30 business days , or have said they tentatively plan to price during the period . 
the supply figure is based on sales data compiled by Reuters Municipal Bond Information Group and uses municipal securities with maturities 13 months or longer . 
all bond deals $ 1 million or larger are included .
