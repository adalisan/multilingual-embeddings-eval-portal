Japan &apos;s corporate service price index ( CSPI ) in July fell 0.9 percent from a year earlier , but was unchanged from a month earlier , the Bank of Japan ( BOJ ) said on Tuesday . 
the overall index stood at 102.1 for July , the same as the previous month . 
the index tracks price movements in the service industry and uses 1990 as its base year .
