German wholesale sales rose a seasonally adjusted 2.5 percent in real terms in January from the previous month , the Federal Statistics Office reported on Friday . 
that compares with a seasonally adjusted decline of 2.4 percent in December . 
calculating the annual change , the statistics office said wholesale sales rose 2.7 percent from January 1996 on an unadjusted basis in real terms . 
in December , wholesale turnover was down 1.7 percent year-on-year . 
the wholesale sales index , seasonally adjusted in real terms , stood at 100.7 in January compared to 98.2 in December and 97.6 in January 1996 .
