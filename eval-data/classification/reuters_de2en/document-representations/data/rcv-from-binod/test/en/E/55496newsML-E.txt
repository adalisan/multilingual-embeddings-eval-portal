Polish consumer prices rose 0.5 percent in August against a 0.1 percent fall in July , bringing year-on-year inflation up to 20.5 percent , the Central Statistical Office ( Gus ) said . 
CPI rose 11.9 percent in the January-August period , compared with a 12.9 percent rise in the same period last year .
