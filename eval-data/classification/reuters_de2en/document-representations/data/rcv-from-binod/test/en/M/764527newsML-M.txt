the Philippine central bank said on Tuesday it had raised its rediscount rate to 12.572 percent for the month of August from 9.969 percent in July . 
the rediscount rate is adjusted every month and calculated one percentage point below the average 91-day Treasury bill rate registered in the last auction of the previous month . 
the rediscount facility is open to all banks which relend the money to exporters and agribusiness entrepreneurs . 
a total of 4.5 billion pesos has been disbursed in the first seven months of the year .
