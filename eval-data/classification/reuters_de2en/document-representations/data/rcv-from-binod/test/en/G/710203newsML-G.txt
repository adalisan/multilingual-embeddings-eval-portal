former Bosnia Croat nationalist leader Mate Boban died in a Mostar hospital from heart failure on Monday after suffering a brain haemorrhage , the Croatian state news agency Hina reported . 
as leader of the Croat community in Bosnia , Boban proclaimed the republic of Herceg-Bosna in August 1993 , complete with Croatian local government , education systems and Croatian currency . 
he was ousted under U.S. pressure when the Moslem-Croat federation was established in 1994 .
