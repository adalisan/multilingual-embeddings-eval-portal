the State ( central ) Bank of Pakistan said on Tuesday it had auctioned six-month maturity short-term federal investment bonds worth 3.277 billion rupees at a weighted average yield of 15.91331 percent per annum . 
the auction will be settled on Thursday , the bank said . 
the bank had set a target of 4.0 billion rupees and received bids worth 3.277 billion rupees , all of which were accepted . 
in the previous auction held on November 19 , bids worth 3.320 billion rupees were accepted at a weighted average yield of 15.10069 percent per annum , against a pre-auction target of 9.526 billion rupees .
