Spanish bank Banco Central Hispano ( BCH ) said on Thursday it forecast Spanish gross domestic product growth of 2.7 percent in 1997 . 
in a note , BCH said Spain &apos;s GDP was likely to show low growth in the first quarter after a truckers &apos; strike and bad weather in January , but it is expected to rebound later in the year reaching a level of 2.9 percent in the last quarter . 
on Wednesday , Economy Secretary Cristobal Montoro said Spain &apos;s 1996 GDP , which rose 2.2 percent during the year and 2.6 percent in the last quarter year-on-year , supported the government forecast for three-percent 1997 growth .
