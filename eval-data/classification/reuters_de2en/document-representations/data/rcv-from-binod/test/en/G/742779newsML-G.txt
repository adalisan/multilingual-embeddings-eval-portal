three people died in a forest fire near the southern Turkish tourist resort of Antalya on Monday , state-run Anatolian news agency said . 
it said the fire in mountain forests north of the city killed one forester as well as an adult and child from the village of Asagi Karaman . 
Antalya mayor Hasan Subasi told NTV news channel on Monday night that the fire had been brought under control near populated areas but was still burning in the deeper forest . 
firefighters , assisted by soldiers and local volunteers were working to extinguish the blaze fanned by winds , he said .
