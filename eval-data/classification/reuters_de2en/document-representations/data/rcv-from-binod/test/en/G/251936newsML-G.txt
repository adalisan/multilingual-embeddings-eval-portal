foreign ministers from the Association of Caribbean States began a two-day meeting in Havana on Thursday to consolidate their two-year-old group . 
in an opening speech , Mexican Foreign Minister Jose Angel Gurria said sectors such as trade , tourism and transport should play leading roles in greater regional cooperation . 
Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina said it was time to take concrete steps toward integration and the organisation should not be allowed to stagnate . 
issues to be discussed at the meeting included a proposal to create a development and cooperation fund among the 25 member nations , which range from tiny island states to larger economies such as Mexico and Venezuela .
